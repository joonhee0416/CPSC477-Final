 2021 Q1 Earnings Call Summary

Havertys reported strong results for the first quarter of 2021, with delivered sales up 31.8% over the prior year quarter and total written sales up 54.5% over the prior year period. The company's gross profit margin increased 160 basis points from 55.5% to 57.1%, and income before income tax increased $23.1 million. Havertys' balance sheet at the end of the first quarter showed inventories up $13.7 million over the December 31, 2020 balance and customer deposits up $18.5 million from the December 31, 2020 balance. The company expects its gross margins for 2021 to be between 56.5% and 57%, with fixed and discretionary type SG&A expenses for 2021 expected to be in the $265 million to $268 million range. Havertys plans to open a new store in Northeast Austin, Texas later this year and is actively investigating and pursuing new store opportunities. The company is also investing in additional warehouse capacities in its distribution network and expects to increase its inventories as the production and product flow improves.
