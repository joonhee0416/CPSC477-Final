 2021 Third Quarter Earnings Call Summary

Centene reported strong third quarter results, with revenue of $32.4 billion, HBR of 88.1%, and adjusted earnings per share of $1.26. The portfolio is performing well, with a strong membership increase in Medicaid and continued growth in Medicare and marketplace. Centene is working closely with states to assess the vaccine and provide support to members. The company is focused on executing its value creation plan and achieving an investment grade rating. Centene expects modest adjusted earnings per share growth in 2022 and will provide more details during its December Investor Day.
