 1. Earnings Call Summary:
INSTACART

Instacart, an online grocery delivery company, reported its first-quarter earnings for 2021. The company's revenue for the quarter was $437.1 million, up 22% from the same period last year. Instacart's gross margin for the quarter was 22.5%, up from 18.8% in the same period last year. The company's net loss for the quarter was $22.6 million, compared to a net loss of $23.5 million in the same period last year. Instacart's total operating expenses for the quarter were $459.7 million, up 25% from the same period last year. The company's adjusted EBITDA for the quarter was $22.5 million, down 29% from the same period last year. Instacart's total revenue for the year is expected to be between $1.75 billion and $1.85 billion.
