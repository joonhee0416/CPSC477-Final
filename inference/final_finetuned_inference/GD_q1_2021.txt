 1. Revenue and Earnings

The company's revenue for the first quarter of 2021 was $640 million higher than the same period last year, resulting in a 7.3% increase. Operating earnings were up $4 million, and net earnings were up $2 million. The defense side of the business saw revenue growth of $444 million, while aerospace revenue increased by $196 million. The operating earnings on the defense side were up 6.4%, while the operating earnings in the aerospace side were down $20 million. The operating margin for the entire company was 10%, which was 70 basis points lower than the year-ago quarter.
