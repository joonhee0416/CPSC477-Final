 2021 Earnings Call Summary for NiSource

NiSource announced its third quarter 2021 earnings, with non-GAAP diluted net operating earnings per share (NOEPS) of $0.11, compared to $0.09 in the third quarter of 2020. The company's gas distribution segment had operating earnings of $18 million, while the electric segment had operating earnings of $130 million. The company's debt level as of June 30 was about $9.6 billion, with a weighted average maturity of approximately 14 years and a weighted average interest rate of approximately 3.7%. NiSource's capital investment estimate for 2021 has been narrowed to approximately $2 billion, and the company's financing plan remains consistent with its earnings growth and credit commitments. The company has updated its 2021 earnings guidance to target the top end of the range of a $1.32 to a $1.36 per share in non-GAAP diluted net operating earnings, and has issued guidance for 2022 of a $1.42 to a $1.48 per share. NiSource's long-term diluted NOEPS guidance is now based on the expected top end of its 2021 guidance range and reaffirms 5% to 7% growth in 2023. The company is also planning to provide an extension to its growth plan at an Investor Day during the first half of next year.
