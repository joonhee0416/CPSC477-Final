 2020 Earnings Call Summary

The company's portfolio performance was strong in 2020, with high occupancy levels, growth in cash rents, and collection of over 99% of build rents. The company delivered year-end occupancy of 95.7%, up 70 basis points from the guidance midpoint provided on the third quarter call. The positive fourth quarter leasing statistics nationally are consistent with the company's experience, as CBRE's preliminary figure for net absorption is 104 million square feet, the highest quarterly result in the last four years and exceeding the 69 million square feet of 4Q completions. The company expects to capitalize on its current land holdings as well as new acquisitions to generate more growth and value creation in 2021. The company is focused on making progress in realizing the three-year cash flow growth opportunity it laid out for investors at its Investor Day in November 2020. As of the call, the company has signed leases for approximately 54% of its 2021 rollovers and a cash rental rate increase of approximately 13%. The company's guidance range for NAREIT FFO is $1.85 to $1.95 per share with a midpoint of $1.90.
