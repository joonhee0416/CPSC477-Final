 1. Financial Results

Quanex reported revenue of $230.1 million in the first quarter of 2021, an increase of 17.1% compared to the same period in 2020. Net income for the quarter was $7.9 million or $0.24 per diluted share, compared to a loss of $10,000 or $0.00 per diluted share in the same period in 2020. On an adjusted basis, net income increased to $9 million or $0.27 per diluted share, and EBITDA increased by 55.4% to $24.3 million.

# 2. Cash Flow and Balance Sheet

Cash used for operating activities was $3.4 million in the first quarter of 2021, and the company's free cash flow was negative. However, the company did not need to borrow on its revolver during the quarter and was able to repay $5 million in bank debt and repurchase approximately $1.9 million of its stock. The balance sheet is strong, and the leverage ratio of net debt to last 12 months adjusted EBITDA is unchanged at 0.6 times.
