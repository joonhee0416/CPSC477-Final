 2021 Third Quarter Earnings Call Summary

Hudson Pacific Properties reported strong financial results for the third quarter of 2021, with robust same property NOI growth and solid leasing activity. The company's focus on top-tier tech and media markets has allowed them to build and reinvest in a state-of-the-art portfolio, while maintaining a strong balance sheet. The advantages of this strategy are more evident than ever, as the pandemic has accelerated the demand for their assets in the tech, media, gaming, and life science industries.

The office leasing market is showing healthy indicators, with a shift away from short-term renewals to new and expansion deals. Sublease space contracted almost without exception as a result of tenants, both pulling listings or backfilling space. The company also saw positive absorption around many of its metro areas in submarkets, and in most cases, for the first time since the pandemic's onset.

Hudson Pacific made several major announcements around the successful growth of their studio platform in the third quarter. They unveiled plans for Sunset Glenoaks, which will be the first purpose-built studio in the Los Angeles area in more than 20 years, growing their studio footprint in that market to a 1.5 million square feet in two stages.
