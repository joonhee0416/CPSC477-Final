 

Applied reported its second quarter earnings, which were characterized by cost discipline, margin control, and solid cash generation. The company's focus on outgrowth potential and cross-selling opportunities remains strong. Applied's balance sheet is in a strong position, with a net leverage of 2.5 times. The company expects sales to be down 2% to flat year-over-year and earnings per share in the range of $4.20 to $4.40 per share. Applied's free cash flow outlook is $200 million to $220 million, which represents a 30% increase from its fiscal 2019. The company is focused on executing its internal growth and margin initiatives, which it believes will support its earnings momentum in coming quarters.
