 2019 Achievements and 2020 Outlook

The transcript discusses the 2019 achievements of Arizona Public Service (APS) and its focus on clean, affordable, reliable, and customer-focused energy. APS has set a goal to deliver 100% clean carbon-free electricity to customers by 2050, with a near-term target of 65% clean energy with 45% coming from renewables by 2030. To achieve this goal, APS plans to acquire resources that appropriately balance reliability, cost, and flexibility for customers. The company also plans to continue developing innovative programs that connect customers with advanced technologies to help manage their bill.

In 2020, APS expects to issue up to $1 billion of term debt at APS and $450 million that Pinnacle West. The company anticipates an effective tax rate in 2020 of 14%, with continued use of income tax incentives reducing cash taxes in the year projects are placed in service. APS' retail customer base grew 2.2% in the fourth quarter of 2019, and the company expects this growth rate to continue in response to the economic trends in its service territory.
