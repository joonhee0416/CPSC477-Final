 2020 Third Quarter Earnings Call Transcript Summary

The transcript provides an overview of the financial performance of Fidelity National Information Services (FNF) for the third quarter of 2020. The company's adjusted pre-tax title earnings were $528 million, a 21.2% increase over the third quarter of 2019, and the adjusted pre-tax title margin was 21.2%, a 260 basis point increase over the prior year quarter. The company's sales engine is also performing well, with consistent net investment spread and earnings, and a strong investment portfolio and capital position. FNF generated $3 billion in total revenue in the third quarter, with the Title segment producing approximately $2.5 billion, F&G producing $442 million, and the Corporate segment generating $50 million. The company's financial results were robust, and it also made strong gains advancing its technology initiatives. FNF's unmatched national footprint and strong local market relationships provide the company with a significant competitive advantage that it can further leverage with technology. The company also announced a quarterly cash dividend of $0.36 per share and plans for targeting $500 million in share repurchases over the next 12 months.
