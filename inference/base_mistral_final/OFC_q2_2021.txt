The company expects to achieve its 1 million square foot growth for the year. The large renewal at DC-6 is still in progress, but the tenant is expected to remain in the building. The company has placed 197,000 square feet of development projects in service, and it expects to deliver two additional projects ahead of schedule later in the year. The company's Defense IT Locations exhibit strength, and it has captured strong demand as shown in its lease accomplishments to date.
