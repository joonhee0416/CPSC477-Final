 2021 Earnings Call Summary

NiSource reported a non-GAAP diluted net operating earnings per share (NOEPS) of $0.11 in the third quarter of 2021, up from $0.09 in the same period last year. The company's gas distribution segment saw an increase in operating revenues and earnings, while the electric segment experienced a slight decrease in operating revenues. NiSource's debt level is approximately $9.2 billion, with a weighted average maturity of 14 years and a weighted average interest rate of 3.7%. The company's capital investment estimate for 2021 is approximately $2 billion, and its financing plan remains consistent with its growth and credit commitments. NiSource has updated its 2021 earnings guidance to target the top end of the range of $1.32 to $1.36 per share in non-GAAP diluted NOEPS, and has issued guidance for 2022 of $1.42 to $1.48. The company plans to provide an extension to its growth plan at an Investor Day during the first half of next year.
