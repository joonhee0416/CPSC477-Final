 1. Revenue and Earnings

The company reported a 7.3% increase in revenue against the first quarter last year, with operating earnings up $4 million and net earnings up $2 million. The operating margin for the entire company was 10%, 70 basis points lower than the year-ago quarter.

# 2. Aerospace and Defense

Revenue on the defense side of the business was up $444 million against the first quarter last year, and aerospace revenue was up $196 million. The operating earnings on the defense side were up $45 million, while operating earnings in the aerospace side were down $20 million.

# 3. Gulfstream

Gulfstream enjoyed revenue of $1.9 billion and operating earnings of $220 million with an 11.7% operating margin. Gulfstream experienced a 5.7% increase in service revenue.

# 4. Jet Aviation

Jet Aviation's FBOs and certain of its maintenance facilities are recovering more slowly. Jet Aviation service revenue is down around $31 million, coupled with a modest increase in service operating earnings.
