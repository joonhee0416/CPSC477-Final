 2020 Earnings Summary

Packaging Corporation of America reported fourth quarter 2020 net income of $124 million or $1.30 per share, excluding special items. The company's total company EBITDA for the fourth quarter, excluding special items, was $293 million. The net sales for the fourth quarter were $1.7 billion in both 2020 and 2019. The full year 2020 earnings excluding special items were $550 million or $5.78 per share, compared to 2019's earnings excluding special items of $726 million or $7.65 per share.

The Packaging business's EBITDA excluding special items in the fourth quarter of 2020 was $303 million with sales of $1.54 billion, resulting in a margin of 20%. The company's full year 2020 Packaging segment EBITDA excluding special items was $1.2 billion with sales of $5.9 billion, or 21% margin.

The company experienced difficulties and unique challenges during the year, including higher freight costs, labor costs, and inventory levels. However, the employees demonstrated tremendous resiliency to overcome adversity and successfully adapted to the needs of customers and managed the market challenges
