 2021 First Quarter Earnings Call Summary

Textron reported strong financial results for the first quarter of 2021, with revenues of $2.9 billion, up from $2.8 billion in the same period last year. The company's net income for the quarter was $0.75 per share, up from $0.35 per share in the first quarter of 2020. Adjusted net income on a non-GAAP measure was $0.70 per share for the first quarter of 2021, compared to $0.35 per share in the first quarter of 2020.

Segment profit in the quarter was $256 million, up from $156 million in the first quarter of 2020. At Bell, revenues were up in the quarter on higher commercial revenues, partially offset by lower military revenues. On future vertical lift, Bell was awarded a contract modification of $293 million for the second phase of the competitive demonstration and reduction program for FLRAA.

Textron Systems revenues were flat in the quarter while the business continued to execute well with improved operating margins. Systems was awarded a contract to up to $607 million from the U.S. Army for the sustainment and modernization of existing shadow systems to the upgraded Block III configuration.
