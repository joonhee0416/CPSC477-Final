 2021 Earnings Call Summary for Graham

Graham reported a solid second quarter, with sales increasing to $28 million, up from $21.6 million in the same period last year. The company's defense market accounted for 50% of the revenue, with a quick-turn material order contributing significantly to the revenue. The company's gross profit margin increased to 27.5%, with EBITDA margins reaching 14.2%. Net income for the quarter was $2.7 million, up from $1.2 million in the same period last year.

Looking ahead, the company expects to see continued growth in the defense market, with a solid pipeline of activity. The company also expects to see a rebound in the crude oil refining and petrochemical markets as demand recovers. The company is also focused on expanding its defense business through M&A activity, with a target of $40 million to $60 million in opportunities identified.

Overall, the company is optimistic about its future prospects and expects to see continued growth in the coming quarters.
