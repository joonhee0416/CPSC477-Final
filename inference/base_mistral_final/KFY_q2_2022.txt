 2022 Korn Ferry Earnings Call Summary

Korn Ferry reported all-time financial performance highs in the second quarter of 2022, with revenue up 47% and diluted and adjusted diluted earnings per share of $1.38 and $1.53, respectively. The company's adjusted EBITDA margin was 21.1%. Korn Ferry's strategy in the New Year will be to continue to innovate, replicate, and scale, with a top-down go-to-market strategy through its Marquee and Regional Accounts. The company will also focus on digital and consulting businesses, marrying Korn Ferry's capabilities with tomorrow's opportunities. Korn Ferry will continue to scale its learning development outsourcing (LDO) capabilities, leveraging its Korn Ferry Advance platform. The company will deploy a balanced capital allocation strategy, including a disciplined approach to M&A. Korn Ferry's performance over recent quarters has reached a new level of scale and agility, and the company is well positioned to help its clients drive performance in this new world.
