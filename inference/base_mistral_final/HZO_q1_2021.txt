 

The earnings call for MarineMax, a leading boat retailer, reported strong first quarter results that exceeded expectations. The company's top priority is the health and safety of its team, customers, and community. Same-store sales increased over 20% in the quarter, and new unit growth was even stronger at 35%. The marine industry is experiencing a significant acceleration in new customers, which is expected to help support future growth. The company has leveraged its investments in technology and added new locations through the acquisition of SkipperBud's. The acquisition has contributed to the company's profitability, with strong gross margins and focused expense management resulting in considerable leverage. The company expects its annual same-store sales growth to be in the high single digits, up from the mid-to-high single digits previously guided. The company is focused on building its strong team culture, executing on its growth strategy, and creating long-term shareholder value.
