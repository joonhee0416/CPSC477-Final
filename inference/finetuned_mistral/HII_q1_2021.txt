"In the first quarter of 2021, HII reported sales of $2.3 billion, which was slightly higher than the same period in 2020. The company's diluted earnings per share for the quarter was $3.68, up from $2.43 in 2020. The pension-adjusted earnings per share was $3.56.

# 2

The company's Technical Solutions business saw a decline in revenues, with a 18.3% decrease from the same period in 2020, mainly due to the divestitures of its oil and gas business and the San Diego shipyard. The Technical Solutions operating income for the quarter was $7 million, a significant improvement from the loss in the first quarter of 2020.

# 3

Ingalls, the company's shipbuilding segment, was awarded a life-cycle engineering and support services contract for the LPD program with a cumulative value of approximately $214 million. The team remains focused on maintaining strong cost and schedule performance in support of their planned production milestones."
