"Taubman Centers, Inc. reported its financial results for the first quarter of 2021. The company's business has significantly improved after the impacts of COVID-19, including governmental orders and capacity restrictions. The company's first-quarter funds from operation were $934 million or $2.48 per share, an increase of approximately $150 million or $0.31 per share compared to the fourth quarter of 2020. The company's operating metrics for the period were as follows: mall and outlet occupancy at the end of the first quarter was 90.8%, down 50 basis points compared to the fourth quarter of 2020. Average base rents was $56.07, up 60 basis points year over year. Leasing momentum has continued across the portfolio, with the company signing 1,100 leases for approximately 4.4 million square feet and having a significant number of leases in its pipeline.
