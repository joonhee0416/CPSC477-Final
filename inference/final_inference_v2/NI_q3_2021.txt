 2021 Earnings Call Summary for NiSource

NiSource is updating its guidance for 2021 to target the top end of the range of a $1.32 to a $1.36 per share in non-GAAP diluted net operating earnings or NOEPS. The company is also initiating guidance for 2022 of a $1.42 to a $1.48, which is consistent with its 5% to 7% near-term growth commitment. NiSource's long-term diluted NOEPS guidance of 7% to 9% through 2024 is now based on the expected top end of its 2021 guidance range, and the company reaffirms 5% to 7% growth in 2023.
