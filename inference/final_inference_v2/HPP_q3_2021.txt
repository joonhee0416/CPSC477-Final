 2021 Third Quarter Earnings Call Summary

Hudson Pacific Properties, Inc. (HPP) reported strong financial performance in the third quarter of 2021, with robust same property NOI growth, positive rent spreads, and a stabilized lease percentage. The company successfully executed on multiple city-related acquisitions and made major announcements around the growth of its studio platform. HPP's combined expertise with Blackstone, across transactions, operations, and development, is second to none and the company is poised to deliver and operate purpose-built next-generation studios. HPP's rent collections remained strong, with 99% for its portfolio overall and 100% for office and studio tenants. The company's balance sheet remains strong, with approximately $0.6 billion in liquidity and no material maturities until 2023. HPP's guidance for full-year 2021 assumes full occupancy and related revenues will not return to pre-COVID condition levels in 2021. The company is narrowing its full-year 2021 guidance in the range of $1.95 to $1.99 per diluted share, excluding specified items, and $0.48 to $0.50 per diluted share, excluding specified items, respectively.
