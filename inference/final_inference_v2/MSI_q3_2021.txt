 2021 Q3 Earnings Call Summary

The company's Q3 results highlight strong demand across the business, with revenue growing 13%, earnings per share 21%, and expanding operating margins by 150 basis points. The company ended the quarter with a record Q3 backlog of $11.4 billion, up 7% from last year. In LMR, revenue was up 11%, while navigating a very challenging supply chain environment, and video security and access control revenue was up 26%, driven by strong broad-based demand for both fixed and mobile video offerings. In Command Center Software, revenue was up 13%. The company is raising its full year guidance for both sales and EPS based on its strong Q3 results and expectations for the remainder of the year. The company's Q3 results included revenue of $2.1 billion, up 13%, including $15 million from acquisitions and $25 million from favorable currency rates. GAAP operating earnings of $451 million and operating margins of 21.4% compared to 18.9% in the year ago quarter.
