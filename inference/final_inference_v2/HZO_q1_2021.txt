 2021 Earnings Call Summary

The transcript highlights the positive results of MarineMax in the first quarter of 2021, with strong growth in same-store sales and new unit growth. The company's gross margin increased due to product margins, growth in storage and service businesses, and the finance and insurance businesses. The acquisition of SkipperBud's and its affiliate Silver Seas Yacht added 20 locations and 35% new unit growth. The balance sheet remains strong, with a current ratio of 1.74 and a total liabilities to tangible net worth ratio of 1.42.

Looking ahead, the company expects to outperform the industry and grow its annual same-store sales growth to be in the high single digits, assuming modest operating leverage improvement above its previous guidance. The guidance uses a share count of about 22.8 million shares and an effective tax rate of 26%. The company remains focused on creating exceptional customer experiences, expanding its brand, and driving cash flow growth.
