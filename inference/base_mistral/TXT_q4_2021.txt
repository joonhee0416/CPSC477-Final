2021 Earnings Summary

Textron reported revenues of $3.3 billion in the fourth quarter of 2021, down from $3.7 billion in the same period last year. The company's adjusted income from continuing operations was $0.94 per share for the fourth quarter of 2021, compared to $1.06 per share in the same period last year. The aviation segment's revenue was $1.2 billion in the fourth quarter of 2021, up from $1.1 billion in the same period last year. The segment's segment profit was $310 million in the fourth quarter of 2021, down from $344 million in the same period last year. The defense segment's revenue was $500 million in the fourth quarter of 2021, up from $480 million in the same period last year.
