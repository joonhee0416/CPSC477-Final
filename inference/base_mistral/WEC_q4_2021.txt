2021 Earnings Summary

The company reported earnings of $4.07 per share, exceeding the upper end of their most recent guidance. The positive results were driven by favorable weather, solid economic recovery in the region, and continued focus on operating efficiency. The balance sheet and cash flows remain strong, allowing the company to fund a highly executable capital plan without issuing equity. The earnings were quality earnings with no adjustments. The company has set ambitious goals to reduce carbon emissions, with a plan to achieve net-zero carbon emissions from power generation by 2050. The company is also actively shaping the future of clean energy and has announced a hydrogen power pilot program. The company is driving forward on its $17.7 billion ESG progress plan, which is focused on efficiency, sustainability, and growth. The plan includes an investment in nearly 2,400 megawatts of renewable capacity over the next five years. The company expects the ESG progress plan to support average growth in its asset base of 7% a year, driving earnings growth, dividend growth, and dramatically improved environmental performance.
