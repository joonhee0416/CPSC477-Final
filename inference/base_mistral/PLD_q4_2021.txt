2021 Earnings Summary for Prologis

Prologis reported a record-setting year in 2021, with core FFO of $4.15 per share and net promote earnings of $0.06. The company's net effective rent change on rollover accelerated to 33%, with a growth rate of 36% in the fourth quarter. The average occupancy rate was 97.4%, and cash same-store NOI growth remained strong at 7.5% for the quarter and 6.1% for the full year.

Prologis' strategic capital business continued to drive tremendous growth in value, with the early wind-up of the UKLV venture contributing $1.7 billion of operating assets to the company's PELF and PELP Ventures. The company raised $4.4 billion of third-party equity in 2021 and has a long development runway of $26 billion.
