 2021 Q1 Earnings Call Summary

The transcript highlights the positive results of the first quarter for Havertys, a furniture retailer. The company experienced a 31.8% increase in delivered sales, with total written sales up 54.5% compared to the prior year period. The gross profit margin increased by 160 basis points, while selling, general and administrative expenses decreased as a percentage of sales. Havertys' financial model has substantial operating leverage at these sales levels, with income before income tax increasing by $23.1 million. The balance sheet shows inventories of $103.6 million and customer deposits of $104.7 million at the end of the quarter. The company expects its gross margins for 2021 to be between 56.5% and 57%, with planned capex of $23 million. Havertys also announced the opening of new stores in Myrtle Beach, South Carolina and Central Florida, with plans for a third store in Northeast Austin, Texas later this year.
