 2021 Watsco Earnings Call Summary

Watsco reported a record-breaking second quarter with earnings per share jumping 64% and sales growing 36%. Gross profits increased by 50% and operating income increased by $88 million. The company's operating margins expanded by 220 basis points to a record 11.7%. The addition of TEC and Acme to the Watsco family has been successful. The company is engaged in a $50 billion North American market and is looking for more companies to join. Watsco's industry-leading technologies continue to gain traction, with customers using the technology growing faster and having lower attrition compared to nonusers. The company's focus remains on long-term growth, and the balance sheet remains in pristine condition with only a small amount of debt.
