 2021 Earnings Call Summary for Textron

In the fourth quarter of 2021, Textron reported revenues of $3.3 billion, a decrease of 11% from the same quarter in 2020. The company's adjusted income from continuing operations was $0.94 per share, an increase of 14% from the same quarter in 2020. Textron's aviation segment had a strong quarter, with segment profit of $310 million, down 14% from the same quarter in 2020. The segment's backlog grew by $655 million in the quarter, resulting in a $4.1 billion backlog at year-end. Textron Systems also had a strong quarter, with a segment margin of 14.8%, up 320 basis points from 2020. The segment delivered the fourth Ship-to-Shore Connector to the U.S. Navy after its successful completion of acceptance trials.

In the defense segment, Textron's aviation business was awarded a $143 million contract for eight AT-6 aircraft, ground support equipment, spare parts, and training from the Royal Thai Air Force. The segment also saw solid commercial order activity, resulting in a segment margin of 10.1% in the fourth quarter
