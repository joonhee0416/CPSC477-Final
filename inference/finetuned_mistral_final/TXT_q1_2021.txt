 2021 Q1 Earnings Call Summary

Textron reported strong first quarter results with revenues of $2.9 billion, up from $2.8 billion in the same quarter last year. The company's net income for the quarter was $0.75 per share, up from $0.35 per share in the same quarter last year. Adjusted net income on a non-GAAP measure was $0.70 per share for the first quarter of 2021, compared to $0.35 per share in the first quarter of 2020.

Segment profit in the quarter was $256 million, up from $156 million in the first quarter of 2020. The manufacturing cash flow before pension contributions totaled $71 million, up $501 million from last year's first quarter.

Bell reported revenues up in the quarter on higher commercial revenues, primarily offset by lower military revenues. The company delivered 17 helicopters up from 15 in the last year's first quarter and saw solid order activity in the quarter across multiple end markets.
