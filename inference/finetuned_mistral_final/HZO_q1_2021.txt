 

The earnings call transcript highlights the strong financial performance of MarineMax in the first quarter of 2021, which exceeded the impressive results from the previous year. The company's top priority is the health and safety of its team, customers, and community as they serve the boating needs of their customers. The marine industry is experiencing a significant acceleration in new customers, which is expected to continue in the future. The company has leveraged its investments in technology to drive leads in marketing analytics, which is converting into sales. The acquisition of SkipperBud's and its affiliate Silver Seas Yacht added 20 locations, including 11 marina and storage operations, and has provided opportunities to share best practices, brands, and resources to drive even greater growth in the years to come. The company's balance sheet is in a strong position, with a current ratio of 1.74 and a total liabilities to tangible net worth ratio of 1.42. The company is raising its earnings per share guidance for 2021 to the range of $4 to $4.20 from its earlier guidance of $3.70 to $3.90, assuming modest operating leverage improvement above its previous guidance. The company is focused on building its strong team culture, executing on its growth strategy, and creating long-term shareholder value.
