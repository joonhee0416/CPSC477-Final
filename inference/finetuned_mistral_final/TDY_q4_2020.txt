 2020 Earnings Summary
Teledyne reported record earnings, operating margin, and cash flow for the full year 2020, despite challenges such as record negative GDP in the second quarter and ongoing challenges faced by manufacturers during the COVID-19 pandemic.

# 2021 Outlook
Teledyne expects a continuing recovery in its commercial businesses and growth in its government businesses in 2021. The company's outlook for the total company's organic growth is between 5% and 6% for 2021, with the strongest growth within the Digital Imaging segment.

# Pending Acquisition of FLIR
Teledyne has been watching FLIR since it entered the Space-based Infrared imaging market in 2006. The company believes that its infrared imaging technologies and market segments are uniquely complementary, and that there is similarity and synergy in digitization, imaging algorithms, machine learning, and other related technologies across each of their organizations.
