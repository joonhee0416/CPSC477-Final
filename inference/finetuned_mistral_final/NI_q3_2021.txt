 2021 Earnings Call Summary for NiSource

NiSource is updating its guidance for 2021, targeting the top end of the range of $1.32 to $1.36 per share in non-GAAP diluted net operating earnings or NOEPS. The company is also initiating guidance for 2022 of $1.42 to $1.48, consistent with its 5% to 7% near-term growth commitment. NiSource's long-term diluted NOEPS guidance of 7% to 9% through 2024 is now based on the expected top end of its 2021 guidance range, and the company reaffirms 5% to 7% growth in 2023.

The company's regulatory execution is progressing, with a proposed order approving a settlement in Pennsylvania, a settlement filed in Kentucky, and a proposed order in Maryland. NiSource filed a gas rate case in Indiana in September. In the third quarter of 2021, the company achieved non-GAAP diluted NOEPS of $0.11, compared to $0.09 in 2020.
