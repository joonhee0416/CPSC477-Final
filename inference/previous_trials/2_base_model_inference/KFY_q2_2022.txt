 2022 Q2 Earnings Call Summary

Korn Ferry reported all-time financial performance highs in Q2 2022. Revenue was up 47%, and diluted and adjusted diluted earnings per share were $1.38 and $1.53, respectively. The adjusted EBITDA margin was 21.1%. Korn Ferry's strategy in the New Year will be to continue to innovate, replicate, and scale, enabling agility in a world that will undoubtedly be in transition for the next several years. Elements of the strategy will include driving a top-down go-to-market strategy, through its Marquee and Regional Accounts, which represent about 36% of the portfolio, and further scaling its learning development outsourcing or LDO capabilities. Korn Ferry will also continue to innovate, marrying its capabilities with tomorrow's opportunities, from ESG to DE&I to M&A services, and further monetize its IP. The company will deploy a balanced capital allocation strategy, including a disciplined approach to M&A. Looking ahead, Korn Ferry has the strategy, the people, the diversity of solutions and expertise to help its clients drive performance in this new world, and its results clearly reflect this reality.
