 

Huntington Ingalls Industries (HII) reported sales of $2.3 billion for the quarter, slightly higher than 2020. Diluted earnings per share for the quarter was $3.68, up from $2.43 in 2020. New contract awards during the quarter were approximately $5.3 billion, resulting in a record backlog of approximately $49 billion, of which approximately $25 billion is funded. The company completed the sale of its oil and gas business and the contribution of the San Diego Shipyard to Titan Acquisition Holdings. The Technical Solutions business is now focused on areas where the company believes its unique capabilities and close customer relationships will drive strong organic revenue growth and margin expansion. The company is confident that its strength, agility, and positive momentum resulting from the impacts of COVID-19 will serve as key catalysts to help it leverage its historic backlog, generate strong free cash flow, and create long-term sustainable value for its shareholders, customers, and employees.
