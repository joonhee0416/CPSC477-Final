 1. Introduction

West Pharmaceuticals is a global healthcare company that provides innovative, high-quality products and solutions for the containment and delivery of injectable medicines. Despite the challenges posed by the COVID-19 pandemic, West remains focused on its mission to improve patient lives and is committed to ensuring the continuity of manufacturing and supply of components and solutions to its customers.

# 2. COVID-19 Challenges

The COVID-19 pandemic has highlighted the importance of global healthcare and the critical role that West plays during these unprecedented times. West's mission to improve patient lives has never been more meaningful than in times like today. The company has taken great pride in its ability to provide innovative, high-quality products and solutions for the containment and delivery of injectable medicines. Despite the challenges posed by the pandemic, West remains focused on its two priorities: the well-being and safety of its team members and the continuity of manufacturing and supply of components and solutions to its customers.
