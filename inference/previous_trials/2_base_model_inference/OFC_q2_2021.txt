 

The company's unique investment strategy of clustering assets adjacent to priority Department of Defense missions is generating strong high-quality earnings. Second quarter FFO per share as adjusted for comparability exceeded the high end of guidance by $0.01, driven primarily by same property results. NOI from real estate operations in the quarter was up 8%, and AFFO increased 17% from a year ago. The company has completed total leasing of 1.7 million square feet, including 1.4 million square feet of renewal leasing and 205,000 square feet of vacancy leasing. The company expects to achieve its 1 million square foot growth for the year, with development leasing activity being a significant driver. The company has a strong set of development and leasing opportunities before it, and the balance sheet and access to capital to seize upon them. The company is increasing its full year guidance from a previously elevated range of $2.19 to $2.25 to a new range of $2.24 to $2.28, with early development deliveries driving most of the increase to guidance.
