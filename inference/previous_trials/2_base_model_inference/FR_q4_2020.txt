 2020 Performance Summary

The FR team delivered outstanding results in 2020, despite the challenging operating environment. The portfolio performance was strong, with high occupancy levels, growth in cash rents, and a collection rate of over 99%. The company grew cash rental rates by 13.5%, the second-highest in the company's history. The positive fourth-quarter leasing statistics nationally reflect the strong demand for high-quality logistics space, with CBRE's preliminary figure for net absorption being 104 million square feet, the highest quarterly result in the last four years.

# 2021 Outlook

The company expects to capitalize on its current land holdings and new acquisitions to generate more growth and value creation in 2021. The company is focused on making progress in realizing the three-year cash flow growth opportunity it laid out at its Investor Day. As of February 11th, 2021, the company has signed leases for approximately 54% of its 2021 rollovers and a cash rental rate increase of approximately 13%. The company expects to achieve a cash rental rate increase of 10% to 14% on new and renewal leasing for the full year 2021.
