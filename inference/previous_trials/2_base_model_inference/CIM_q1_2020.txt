 2020 Q1 Earnings Call Summary for Chimera

Chimera, a financial services company, reported its Q1 2020 financial results. The company's GAAP book value was $12.45 per share, and its GAAP net loss for the quarter was $389 million or $2.08 per share. However, on a core basis, the company's net income for the quarter was $106 million or $0.57 per share. The company's economic net interest income for the quarter was $151 million, with a yield on average interest-earning assets of 5.3% and an average cost of funds of 3%. Its net interest spread was 2.3%. The total leverage for the quarter was 4.7 to 1, while the recourse leverage ended at 2.2 to 1. The company's expenses for the quarter, excluding servicing fees and transaction expenses, were $18.6 million, consistent with the previous quarter. The company had approximately $650 million in cash and unencumbered assets after paying its preferred and common stock dividends of $111 million and closing a public convertible bond offering that raised $374 million.
