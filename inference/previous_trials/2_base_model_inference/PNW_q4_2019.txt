 2019 APS Earnings Call Summary

APS, Arizona Public Service, is a company that provides electricity services to customers in Arizona. The company's strategy is anchored by four concepts: clean, affordable, reliable, and customer-focused. The company plans to deliver 100% clean carbon-free electricity to customers by 2050, with a near-term target of 65% clean energy with 45% coming from renewables by 2030 and a commitment to exit coal by 2031. To support the affordability of the transition to a carbon-free resource mix, the company will have a sharp focus on economic development in Arizona, growing its customer base, and allocating costs across more customers, which helps keep rates affordable and increase shareholder value. The company plans to continue developing innovative programs that connect customers with advanced technologies to help manage their bill. The company also plans to convene an advisory panel of customers to gain a deeper understanding of the customer experience through individual perspective, so a little design basis thinking. The company plans to continue working collaboratively with those who have vested interest in Arizona's future and its role as the state's largest electricity provider.
