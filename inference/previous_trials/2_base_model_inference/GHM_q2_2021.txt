 

Graham Corporation reported its Fiscal 2021 Second Quarter Results, with sales increasing to $28 million, up from $21.6 million in the same period last year. Defense sales were $9.4 million in the quarter, with a quick turn material order making up approximately half of the revenue. The company's backlog improved to $114.9 million, with 60% to 65% of backlog planned to convert during the next four quarters. The company's gross profit increased to $7.7 million, with gross margins of 27.5%, and EBITDA margins of 14.2%. The company's net income was $2.7 million, up from $1.2 million last year. The company expects to increase its revenue range to between $93 million and $97 million for the full fiscal year, with gross margins expected to be between 21% and 23%. The company's SG&A spend is projected to be between $17 million and $17.5 million, with an effective tax rate of approximately 22%.
