 2021 Earnings Summary

Textron reported revenues of $3.3 billion in the fourth quarter of 2021, down from $3.7 billion in the same period last year. The company recorded $5 million in pre-tax special charges related to its 2020 restructuring plan, resulting in adjusted income from continuing operations of $0.94 per share for the fourth quarter of 2021, up from $1.06 per share in the same period last year. Segment profit in the quarter was $310 million, down $14 million from the same period last year. For the full year, revenues were $12.4 billion, up $731 million from the previous year. The company delivered 167 jets and 125 commercial turboprops in 2021, up from 132 jets and 113 commercial turboprops in 2020. The company's aviation segment achieved a segment margin of 10.1% in the fourth quarter, up from 9.1% in the same period last year. The company's defense segment also saw solid performance, with revenues of $858 million in the fourth quarter, up from $814 million in the same period last year.
