 

Quanex reported strong financial results for the first quarter of 2021, with revenue increasing by 17.1% compared to the same period last year. The increase in revenue was primarily driven by increased demand for their products across all product lines and operating segments. Net income for the quarter was $7.9 million or $0.24 per diluted share, compared to $10,000 or $0.00 per diluted share during the three months ended January 31, 2020. On an adjusted basis, EBITDA increased by 55.4% to $24.3 million, compared to $15.7 million during the same period of last year. The company expects to generate revenue growth of approximately 12% in their North American fenestration segment, approximately 5% in their North American cabinet components segment and approximately 22% in their European fenestration segment for the full year 2021. The company's balance sheet is strong, with a liquidity position that is solid and a leverage ratio of net debt to last 12 months adjusted EBITDA that is unchanged at 0.6 times as of January 31, 2021.
