"0 Earnings Summary for Chemed Corporation

Chemed Corporation, a leading provider of hospice care and plumbing services, reported its third quarter 2020 operating results. The company's hospice segment operations continued to be impacted by the pandemic, but admissions have increased since the second quarter, reflecting the relaxation of regulations and increased flexibility in caring for patients. Roto-Rooter services demand has shown significant improvement since the second quarter, driven by increased commercial and residential demand. Chemed's full year 2020 revenue is estimated to be $18 to $18.15 per diluted share, excluding non-cash expenses and other items. The company's full year 2020 revenue growth is forecasted to be 12.5% to 13%, assuming 2.7% sequential growth from the third quarter to the fourth quarter."
