 1. Financial Summary

The company reported sales of $2.3 billion for the quarter, which were slightly higher than 2020. Diluted earnings per share were $3.68 for the quarter and pension adjusted earnings per share was $3.56, up from $2.43 in 2020. The company also had a record backlog of approximately $49 billion, of which approximately $25 billion is funded.

# 2. Contract Awards

The company was awarded a life-cycle engineering and support services contract for the LPD program with a cumulative value of approximately $214 million. The team also won a contract for the DDG program, which has a total value of $1.6 billion.

# 3. Portfolio Shaping Actions

The company completed the sale of its oil and gas business and also contributed the San Diego Shipyard to Titan Acquisition Holdings in exchange for a noncontrolling interest in this leading provider of ship repair and fleet sustainment services. This sharpened the focus of the Technical Solutions business into areas where the company believes its unique capabilities and close customer relationships will drive strong organic revenue growth and margin expansion.

# 4. Operational Highlights

Ingalls achieved a 25% complete milestone on LHA eight Bougainville during the quarter, and the team remains focused on maintaining strong cost and schedule performance in support of their planned production milestones. On the DDG program, the team is preparing for the launch of DDG 125.
