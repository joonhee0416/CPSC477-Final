 1. Applied Financial Summary

Applied Financial Summary is a company that provides technical solutions and services to its customers. The company has been in business for over 100 years and is a leading provider of motion control, fluid power, flow control, and automation solutions. The company's strategy is to provide its customers with the best technical solutions and services, and it has been successful in this regard.

Applied Financial Summary's financial performance for the second quarter of 2020 was characterized by cost discipline, margin control, and solid cash generation. The company's EBITDA in the quarter was $74.5 million, and its EBITDA margin was 8.9%. The company's reported earnings per share for the quarter were $0.97 per share.

The company's cash generated from operating activities was $34.9 million, and its free cash flow was $47.9 million. The company's year-to-date free cash flow of $93 million represents 119% of adjusted net income and is up nearly 60% from the prior year.

Applied Financial Summary's outlook for the second half of 2020 is cautious. The company expects sales to be down 2% to flat year-over-year or down 5% to down 3% on an organic day per day basis. The company also expects earnings per share in the range of $4.20 to $4.40 per share.

The company's free cash flow outlook for the full year is $200 million to $220 million, which represents a 30% increase from its fiscal 2019 at the midpoint.
