 2021 Earnings Call Transcript Summary

NiSource reported a net operating earnings of $47 million or $0.11 per diluted share in the third quarter of 2021, a 20% increase from the same period in 2020. The company's gas distribution segment had operating earnings of $18 million, a 143% increase from the same period in 2020, while the electric segment had operating earnings of $130 million, a 3% decrease from the same period in 2020. NiSource's debt level as of June 30 was $9.6 billion, with a weighted average maturity of approximately 14 years and a weighted average interest rate of approximately 3.7%. The company's capital investment estimate for 2021 has been narrowed to approximately $2 billion, with a forecast of $2.4 billion to $2.7 billion for 2022. NiSource has updated its 2021 earnings guidance to target the top end of the range of a $1.32 to a $1.36 per share in non-GAAP diluted net operating earnings or NOEPS. The company has also initiated guidance for 2022 of a $1.42 to a $1.48 and reaffirmed its long-term diluted NOEPS guidance of 7% to 9% through 2024.
