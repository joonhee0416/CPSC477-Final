 2021 Earnings Summary for Prologis

Prologis reported record-setting activity across its business in 2021, with core FFO of $4.15 per share and net promote earnings of $0.06. The company's net effective rent change on rollover accelerated to 33%, with a growth rate of over 37% in the US. Average occupancy was 97.4%, up 80 basis points sequentially. Cash same-store NOI growth remained strong at 7.5% for the quarter and 6.1% for the full year. Prologis has modified its in-place to market rent disclosure to standardize this metric among logistics REITs. The company's strategic capital business continues to drive tremendous growth in value, with development starts totaling $3.6 billion with margins of 32%. Stabilizations totaled $2.5 billion with estimated value creation of $1.3 billion and average margin of 53%, both all-time highs. Realized development gains were $817 million for the year, also an all-time high. The company expects cash same-store NOI growth to range between 6% and 7% and average occupancy to range between 96.5% to 97.5% for 2022. It is forecasting rent growth in its markets to be 11% in the US and 10% globally. Prologis expects revenue, excluding promotes, to range between $540 million and $560 million and net promote income of $0.55 per share for the year.
