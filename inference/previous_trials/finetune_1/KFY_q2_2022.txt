 1

Korn Ferry reported all-time financial performance highs in the second quarter, with revenue up 47% and diluted and adjusted diluted earnings per share of $1.38 and $1.53, respectively. The adjusted EBITDA margin was 21.1%. The company's strategy in the New Year will be to continue to innovate, replicate, and scale, allowing people and organizations to exceed their potential in this rapidly changing world.

# 2

The company's search businesses, both Pro Search and Executive Search, represent about 45% of its revenue, and there is still substantial market opportunity ahead, given the acceleration of an increasingly nomadic labor market. The acquisition of the Lucas Group during the quarter added breadth and depth to Korn Ferry's search portfolio.

# 3

Korn Ferry's digital and consulting businesses will continue to innovate, marrying its capabilities with tomorrow's opportunities. The company will further push the monetization of its IP and move more of its digital business to a subscription offering. It will also scale its learning development outsourcing or LDO capabilities, leveraging its Korn Ferry Advance platform, in which it has completed 50,000 development and coaching sessions.

# 4

Korn Ferry will deploy a balanced capital allocation strategy, including a disciplined approach to M&A. The company's results clearly reflect its strategy, people, diversity of solutions, and expertise to help its clients drive performance in this new world.
