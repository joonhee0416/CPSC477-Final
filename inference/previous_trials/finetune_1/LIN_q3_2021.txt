 2021 Q3 Earnings Call Summary

Linde delivered another strong quarter in Q3 2021, with record operating cash flow and return on capital. The company's project backlog nearly doubled, providing a solid foundation for high-quality, long-term growth. Sanjiv will become CEO effective March 1st, and Steve will continue to provide guidance as a Director and significant shareholder. The company's focus will be on profitable growth, optimizing the business, cash generation, and ROC. Linde's project backlog increased from $7.5 billion to $13.4 billion, with underlying sales growing 11% over the prior year. The company's operating margin was 23.6%, with a 16.7% return on capital. The company generated a record level of $2.6 billion in operating cash in Q3 2021, with $2.3 billion invested in the business and $4.8 billion distributed back to shareholders. The company has access to very attractive capital through the debt markets, with a high degree of confidence to maintain shareholder-friendly allocation policies over the long term. The company's full-year guidance for 2021 is $10.52 to $10.62, representing a growth rate of 28% to 29% over 2020 and 43% to 45% over 2019.
