 1

Graham Corporation reported its fiscal 2021 second quarter results, with sales of $28 million, up from $21.6 million in the same period last year. Defense sales accounted for $9.4 million of the total sales, and the company expects defense sales to be around 25% of its sales for the full fiscal year. The quarter benefited from an acceleration of work at one of its Asian subcontractors, which pulled some work from Q3 into Q2. The company's net income was $2.7 million or $0.27 per share, up from $1.2 million or $0.12 per share last year.

# 2

For the first half of fiscal 2021, Graham Corporation's sales were $44.7 million, compared with $42.2 million in the first half of last year. The company's gross profit was $9.3 million, slightly off the $9.7 million last year, and gross margins were down 20.7% versus 22.9% last year. Year-to-date EBITDA margins were 4.9% versus 4.5% in the first half of last year, and net income was $900,000 or $0.09 per share, down from $1.3 million or $0.13 per share last year.

# 3

Graham Corporation's cash is at $67.9 million, down from $73 million at the end of fiscal 2020, but this is simply timing of working capital, namely accounts receivable and accounts payable. The company expects this to come back in the third quarter. The company's quarterly dividend remains firm at $0.11 a share. Capital
