 1. Revenue and Net Income

Quanex reported revenue of $230.1 million in the first quarter of 2021, a 17.1% increase compared to the same period in 2020. Net income for the quarter was $7.9 million or $0.24 per diluted share, an increase of 55.4% compared to the same period in 2020.

# 2. EBITDA and Margin

Adjusted EBITDA for the quarter increased by 55.4% to $24.3 million, and the adjusted EBITDA margin expanded by approximately 260 basis points.

# 3. Cash Flow and Balance Sheet

Cash used for operating activities was $3.4 million during the three months ended January 31, 2021, and the company did not need to borrow on its revolver during the quarter. The balance sheet is strong, and the liquidity position is solid. The leverage ratio of net debt to last 12 months adjusted EBITDA is unchanged at 0.6 times.

# 4. Segment Performance

The North American fenestration segment generated revenue of $128.1 million in Q1, a 17.7% increase compared to prior-year Q1. The European fenestration segment generated revenue of $49.1 million in the first quarter, a 34% increase compared to the prior year. The North American cabinet components segment reported net sales of $54 million in Q1, an 8% increase compared to the prior year.
