 2021 Earnings Summary

Textron reported revenues of $3.3 billion in the fourth quarter of 2021, a decrease of $400 million from the same quarter in 2020. The company's adjusted income from continuing operations was $0.94 per share for the fourth quarter of 2021, a decrease of $0.07 per share from the same quarter in 2020. Segment profit for the quarter was $310 million, a decrease of $14 million from the same quarter in 2020.

## Aviation

In the aviation segment, revenues were $2.2 billion, an increase of $100 million from the same quarter in 2020. The segment's profit margin was 7.2%, an increase of 0.8 percentage points from the same quarter in 2020. The segment delivered 167 jets in 2021, an increase of 32 jets from the same year in 2020.

## Defense

In the defense segment, revenues were $1.1 billion, a decrease of $100 million from the same quarter in 2020. The segment's profit margin was 10.1%, an increase of 0.9 percentage points from the same quarter in 2020. The segment delivered 125 commercial turboprops in 2021, an increase of 12 turboprops from the same year in 2020.
