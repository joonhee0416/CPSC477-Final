MarineMax reported strong first quarter results, with continued growth in same-store sales and new unit growth. The company's gross margin expanded due to increases in product margins, growth in storage and service businesses, and focused expense management. The acquisition of SkipperBud's added 20 locations and improved the company's balance sheet. The company expects to achieve high single-digit same-store sales growth in 2021, with a guidance range of $4 to $4.20 for earnings per share. The company is focused on building its strong team culture, executing on its growth strategy, and creating long-term shareholder value.
