Linde reported another solid quarter of record earnings per share performance, despite increasingly challenging market conditions. The company's project backlog nearly doubled, providing a strong foundation for high quality, long term growth. Linde is committed to reducing its greenhouse gas emissions footprint and has a roadmap in place to achieve this goal. The company's operating culture is focused on continuous improvement across key operating metrics. Linde's CEO, Steve, will become the Chairman of the Board, while Sanjiv will become CEO effective March first. Sanjiv has demonstrated his ability to lead the company and has been appointed through a diligent three year succession planning process. Linde's growth initiatives include securing project backlog, accretive base capex investments, and strength in low capital intensity areas such as healthcare, food and beverage, engineering, and packaged gas business. The company's project backlog has increased from $7.5 billion to $13.4 billion sequentially, and underlying sales grew 11% over prior year and 1% sequentially.
