2019 APS Earnings Call Summary

APS, a leading electricity provider in Arizona, announced its 2019 achievements and provided updates on its operating and regulatory plans. The company's strategy is anchored by four concepts: clean, affordable, reliable, and customer-focused. APS aims to deliver 100% clean carbon-free electricity to customers by 2050, with a near-term target of 65% clean energy with 45% coming from renewables by 2030. The company plans to acquire resources that balance reliability, cost, and flexibility for customers while maintaining affordability. APS also plans to continue developing innovative programs that connect customers with advanced technologies to help manage their bill and convene an advisory panel of customers to gain a deeper understanding of the customer experience. The company expects to issue equity sometime after 2020, with an expected amount in the $300 million to $400 million range.
