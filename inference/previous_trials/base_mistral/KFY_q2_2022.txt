Korn Ferry reported all-time financial performance highs in the second quarter, with revenue up 47% and diluted and adjusted diluted earnings per share of $1.38 and $1.53, respectively. The adjusted EBITDA margin was 21.1%. The company's strategy in the New Year will be to continue to innovate, replicate, and scale, enabling agility in a rapidly changing world. Elements of the strategy will include driving a top-down go-to-market strategy, through the Marquee and Regional Accounts, which represent about 36% of the portfolio. The company will also continue to innovate, marrying Korn Ferry's capabilities with tomorrow's opportunities, from ESG to DE&I to M&A services and further monetization of its IP. The company will also scale its learning development outsourcing or LDO capabilities, leveraging its Korn Ferry Advance platform. The company will deploy a balanced capital allocation strategy, including a disciplined approach to M&A. Looking ahead, the company believes it has the strategy, the people, the diversity of solutions and expertise
