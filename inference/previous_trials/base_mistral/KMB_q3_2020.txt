Kimberly-Clark Corporation (KCC) reported its third quarter 2020 financial results, with organic sales increasing 3% and achieving cost savings. The company's adjusted gross margin was 36.2%, up 40 basis points year-on-year, and adjusted earnings per share were $1.72, compared to $1.84 in the year-ago period. KCC expects to raise its full-year outlook, with organic sales growth of 5% compared to its prior target of 4% to 5%. The company's recent acquisition of Softex is expected to improve its underlying growth prospects and expand its presence in high-growth markets.
