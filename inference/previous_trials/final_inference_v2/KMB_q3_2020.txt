 2021 Third Quarter Earnings Call Summary

Kimberly-Clark Corporation (K-C) reported its third quarter 2021 financial results, with organic sales increasing 3% and a strong quarter of achieving cost savings. The company's adjusted gross margin was 36.2%, up 40 basis points year-on-year, and adjusted operating profit was down 6% with an operating margin of 17.2%. The company's full-year outlook was raised, with organic sales growth of 5% expected compared to its previous target of 4% to 5%. K-C is investing in its brands and improving its market positions, and the acquisition of Softex Indonesia is expected to improve its underlying growth prospects. The company is optimistic about its opportunities to generate long-term growth and create shareholder value.
