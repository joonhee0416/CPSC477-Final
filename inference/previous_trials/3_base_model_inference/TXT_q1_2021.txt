 2021 Q1 Earnings Summary

Textron reported strong financial performance in the first quarter of 2021, with revenues of $2.9 billion, up from $2.8 billion in the same period last year. Net income was $0.75 per share, up from $0.35 per share in the first quarter of 2020. Adjusted net income on a non-GAAP measure was $0.70 per share for the first quarter of 2021, compared to $0.35 per share in the first quarter of 2020. The adjusted net income for 2021 excludes $6 million of pre-tax special charges, $0.02 per share after tax related to the 2020 restructuring plan, and $15 million of pre-tax gain on the sale of TRU Canada, $0.07 per share after tax.
