 2021 L3Harris Q1 Earnings Call Summary

L3Harris reported strong first quarter results, building on last year's progress with solid execution across all financial metrics. Organic revenue is up approximately 2% with mid-single-digit growth in the core government businesses, partially offset by COVID-related impacts of about three points from the commercial businesses. The company expects more stability in the affected businesses ahead. Margins expanded a robust 140 basis points to 18.9%, resulting in earnings per share of $3.18, up 14% and ahead of internal targets. Free cash flow was $630 million supported by shareholder returns in excess of $900 million, including repurchases of $700 million from the recently authorized $6 billion program, with a balance from dividends following the 20% increase in March. The company is excited and optimistic about what L3Harris can accomplish in its next phase under Chris' leadership.
