 1. Q3 2021 Earnings Call Summary

Hudson Pacific Properties, Inc. (HPP) reported strong financial performance in the third quarter of 2021, with robust same-property NOI growth and positive rent spreads. The company's focus on top-tier tech and media markets has allowed it to maintain a strong balance sheet and build a state-of-the-art portfolio. HPP's stabilized lease percentage remains over 92%, and it successfully executed on multiple city-related acquisitions despite the pandemic headwinds.

# 2. Key Highlights

- Robust same-property NOI growth: HPP's office and studio tenants continue to pay rent and repay deferred rent, resulting in a 5.1% increase in third-quarter NOI on a GAAP basis and a 10.8% increase on a cash basis for its 45 consolidated same-store office properties. For its three same-store studio properties, NOI increased by 49.8% on a GAAP basis and 45.5% on a cash basis.
