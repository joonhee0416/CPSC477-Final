 2021 Second Quarter Earnings Call Summary for Graham

Graham reported a strong second quarter with $28 million in sales, up from $21.6 million in the same period last year. The Navy market accounted for $9.4 million of the quarter's sales, with a quick turn material order making up approximately half of the revenue. Defense sales were $12.9 million or 29% of total sales, slightly higher than expected for the full year. The quarter also benefited from an acceleration of work at one of the company's Asian subcontractors. Net income for the quarter was $2.7 million or $0.27 per share, up from $1.2 million or $0.12 per share in the same period last year. The gross profit for the quarter was $7.7 million, up from $4.9 million in the same period last year, primarily due to volume. Gross margins were 27.5%, up from 22.9%.
