 2020 Q2 Earnings Call Summary

Applied reported its second quarter financial results, highlighting cost discipline, margin control, and solid cash generation in a soft end market. Despite declines in organic sales, particularly in metals, mining, oil and gas, machinery, and process-related industries, the company remained focused on accelerating its growth potential through its leading technical position and service capabilities. Applied also entered the second half of fiscal 2020 with a strong balance sheet, having reduced outstanding debt by $115 million over two years. The company expects sales to be down 2% to flat year-over-year, with earnings per share in the range of $4.20 to $4.40 per share. Applied reaffirmed its free cash flow outlook of $200 million to $220 million, representing a 30% increase from its fiscal 2019 at the midpoint.
