 2021 Third Quarter Earnings Call Summary

Centene anticipates that subsequent events and developments may cause its estimates to change. The company will invite sell-side analysts to participate in person in New York, and ask others to participate virtually at the upcoming 2022 guidance meeting on December 10. Brent Layton has a conflict that could not be avoided, but will join the call in the future.

In the third quarter, Centene generated revenue of $32.4 billion and HBR of 88.1% and adjusted earnings per share of $1.26. The numbers reflect a return to a normal utilization while still covering reasonable amounts of COVID costs which seem to have peaked in August. The company's performance provides a strong foundation for its value creation plan, and it remains committed to its margin goals.

Centene is working closely with states and the timing redetermination, which has been extended until January with the opportunity for additional extensions three months at a time.
