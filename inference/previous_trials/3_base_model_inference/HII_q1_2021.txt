 2021 Q1 Earnings Call Summary

In the first quarter of 2021, the company reported $2.3 billion in sales, which was slightly higher than the same period in 2020. Diluted earnings per share for the quarter was $3.68, and pension-adjusted earnings per share was $3.56, up from $2.43 in 2020. The company also had a record backlog of approximately $49 billion, of which approximately $25 billion is funded.

During the quarter, the company was awarded several contracts, including a life-cycle engineering and support services contract for the LPD program with a cumulative value of approximately $214 million. The team was also focused on preparations for the launch of DDG 125.
