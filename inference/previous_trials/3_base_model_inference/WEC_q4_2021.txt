 2021 Earnings Summary

The company reported earnings that exceeded the upper end of its most recent guidance, driven by favorable weather, solid economic recovery, and continued focus on operating efficiency. The balance sheet and cash flows remain strong, allowing the company to fund a highly executable capital plan without issuing equity. The earnings are quality earnings with no adjustments. The company has set aggressive goals to reduce carbon emissions, with a plan to achieve net-zero carbon emissions from power generation by 2050. The company is also actively shaping the future of clean energy, with a focus on hydrogen as a key player in decarbonizing the economy. The company is driving forward on its $17.7 billion ESG progress plan, with a focus on efficiency, sustainability, and growth, including the planned investment in nearly 2,400 megawatts of renewable capacity over the next five years. The company expects the ESG progress plan to support average growth in its asset base of 7% a year, driving earnings growth, dividend growth, and dramatically improved environmental performance.
