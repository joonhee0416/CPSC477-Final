 

The transcript discusses the financial results of First National of Title Information Services (FNF) for the third quarter of 2020. The company generated record adjusted pre-tax title earnings of $528 million, a 21.2% adjusted pre-tax title margin, and $3 billion in total revenue. FNF's acquisition of FGL Holdings, F&G, has been successful, with F&G producing excellent sales results and entering into a mutually beneficial annuity flow reinsurance partnership. The company also streamlined its operations and sold F&G Reinsurance to a third-party offshore reinsurance platform. FNF remains committed to creating meaningful long-term value for its shareholders through its capital allocation strategy, including a quarterly cash dividend of $0.36 per share and plans for targeting $500 million in share repurchases. The company's title claims paid were $27 million lower than its provision for the quarter, and its title investment portfolio totaled $5.3 billion at September 30.
