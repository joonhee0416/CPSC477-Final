 2021 Earnings Call Summary for NiSource

NiSource, a leading energy company, announced its third-quarter 2021 financial results and provided an update on its long-term growth plans. The company's non-GAAP diluted net operating earnings per share (NOEPS) for the third quarter were $0.11, compared to $0.09 in the same period last year. NiSource's gas operations continue to progress, with pending rate cases in Ohio, Pennsylvania, and Kentucky. The company expects to achieve its 2021 guidance range of a $1.32 to a $1.36 per share in non-GAAP diluted NOEPS. NiSource's long-term growth commitment is to achieve a diluted NOEPS growth rate of 7% to 9% through 2024. The company is also planning to retire remaining coal-fired generation between 2026 and 2028 and reduce greenhouse gas emissions 90% by 2030.
