 

Korn Ferry reported exceptional financial performance in the second quarter of 2021, with revenue up 47% and diluted and adjusted diluted earnings per share of $1.38 and $1.53, respectively. The adjusted EBITDA margin was 21.1%. The company's strategy in the New Year will focus on innovation, replication, and scaling to help clients drive performance in the new world. Korn Ferry will continue to invest in its digital and consulting businesses, including expanding its team of consultants in the second quarter. The company expects its consolidated fee revenue in the third quarter of fiscal '22 to range from $640 million to $660 million and its consolidated adjusted diluted earnings per share to range from $1.42 to $1.58 while its GAAP diluted earnings per share should range from $1.38 to $1.56. Korn Ferry has never been better positioned to serve all of its constituencies and is well positioned to take more than its fair share of the growing market.
