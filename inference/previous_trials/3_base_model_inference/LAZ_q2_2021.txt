 1. Financial Summary

In the second quarter of 2021, Lazard reported record operating revenue of $821 million, up 51% from the same period last year. The company's financial advisory revenue increased by 61% in the second quarter, driven by broad-based activity across sectors, market cap, and regions. The Global Private Capital Advisory franchise had a strong quarter, serving financial sponsors with new fundraising and innovative secondary market solutions. Asset Management operating revenue reached an all-time high of $343 million in the second quarter, up 40% from the same period last year. The increase in AUM was primarily driven by market appreciation and positive foreign exchange movement, with $0.8 billion of net outflows. Lazard continues to invest in people, technology, and distribution effort, as well as the development of new and existing funds and the scaling up of its platforms.
