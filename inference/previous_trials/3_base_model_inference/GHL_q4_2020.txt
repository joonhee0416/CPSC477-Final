 

In the fourth quarter of 2020, Greenhill reported revenue of $140.7 million, an operating margin of 57%, and net income of $2.71 per share. For the year, the company had revenue of $311.7 million, an operating margin of 18%, and net income of $1.36 per share. Greenhill's quarterly revenue was up 32%, and earnings per share was up 158% from the same period last year. The company's full-year revenue was up 4%, and earnings per share was up 202% from the prior year. Greenhill's strong quarter was not simply a matter of transaction timing, as the earnings per share for the quarter was greater than the analyst consensus forecast for the fourth quarter and the next four quarters ahead combined. The company's European M&A business and its U.S. restructuring business contributed significantly to its revenue, as did an expanding array of financing advisory roles that are neither traditional M&A nor traditional restructuring.
