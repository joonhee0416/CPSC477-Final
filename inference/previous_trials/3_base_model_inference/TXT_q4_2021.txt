 2021 Earnings Summary

Textron reported its fourth quarter and full-year earnings for 2021. Revenue for the quarter was $3.3 billion, down from $3.7 billion in the previous quarter. The company recorded a pre-tax special charge of $5 million related to its 2020 restructuring plan. Excluding these charges, adjusted income from continuing operations was $0.94 per share for the quarter, up from $1.06 per share in the previous quarter. Segment profit in the quarter was $310 million, down from $345 million in the previous quarter.

In the aviation segment, the company delivered 167 jets and 125 commercial turboprops in 2021, up from 132 jets and 113 commercial turboprops in 2020.
