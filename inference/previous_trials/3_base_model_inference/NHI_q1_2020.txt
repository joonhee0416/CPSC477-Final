 

In summary, NHI's earnings call for the quarter ended March 31, 2020, focused on the impact of the COVID-19 pandemic on the senior housing and skilled nursing industries. The company's operators have done an admirable job in limiting the spread of the virus, but this has resulted in a significant downturn in inquiries, tours, and move-ins, leading to a negative impact on occupancy. On the cost side, operators are spending more, particularly for labor and PPE suppliers, which is pressuring their margins. NHI has received 99.7% of its contractual rent in April and collected approximately 94% in May, in line with expectations. The company is committed to transparency with the investment community and has enhanced occupancy disclosure on Bickford, Holiday, and Senior Living Communities. NHI has limited visibility to the financial impact of the COVID-19 pandemic and has withdrawn its previously issued 2020 guidance.
