 

Watsco has reported a successful second quarter, with earnings per share jumping 64% to $3.71 per share and gross margins expanding 220 basis points to a record 11.7%. Sales grew 36% to $1.85 billion, and operating income increased $88 million or 68% to $217 million. The company has recently acquired two new companies, TEC and Acme, and is looking to find more companies to join its $50 billion North American market. Watsco's industry-leading technologies continue to gain traction, with growth rates among active users of its technologies outpacing nonusers and attrition among customers using its technology being lower compared to nonusers. The company's focus remains on long-term growth, and it has a balance sheet in pristine condition with only a small amount of debt.
