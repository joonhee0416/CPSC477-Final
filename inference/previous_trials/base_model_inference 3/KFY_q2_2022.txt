 2021 Korn Ferry Earnings Call Summary

Korn Ferry reported all-time financial performance highs in Q2 2021, with revenue up 47% and diluted and adjusted diluted earnings per share of $1.38 and $1.53, respectively. The adjusted EBITDA margin was 21.1%. The company's strategy for 2022 includes innovating, replicating, and scaling, driving a top-down go-to-market strategy, and leveraging its Korn Ferry Advance platform. Korn Ferry also plans to continue investing in consultants and execution staff and expects to maintain strong G&A spend in the third quarter. The company expects its consolidated fee revenue in the third quarter of fiscal '22 to range from $640 million to $660 million and its consolidated adjusted diluted earnings per share to range from $1.42 to $1.58 while its GAAP diluted earnings per share should range from $1.38 to $1.56.
