 2021 Earnings Summary for Prologis

Prologis reported record-setting activity across its business in 2021. Core FFO was $4.15 per share, with net promote earnings of $0.06. Excluding promotes, core FFO grew 14% year over year. The net effective rent change on rollover accelerated to 33%, up 510 basis points sequentially, and was led by the U.S. at over 37%. Average occupancy was 97.4%, up 80 basis points sequentially. Cash same-store NOI growth remained strong at 7.5% for the quarter and 6.1% for the full year.

Prologis has modified its in-place to market rent disclosure to standardize this metric among logistics REITs. Using this new definition consistently applied, the net effective lease mark-to-market at year-end jumped almost 800 basis points sequentially to 36%. This current rent spread represents embedded organic NOI of more than $1.2 billion or $1.55 per share that will be captured without any further market rent growth.

Prologis has a highly disciplined team and an incredibly strong operating environment. The company raised $4.4 billion of third-party equity in 2021 and has a long development runway with a land portfolio able to support $26 billion of future starts. Realized development gains were $817 million for the year, also an all-time high.

Prologis has a unique position to help its customers address current supply chain challenges. The company has shortened construction delivery times by navigating raw material shortages and leveraging its Essentials platform to procure warehouse equipment and services.
