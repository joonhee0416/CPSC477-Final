 2021 Earnings Call Summary

NiSource announced its third quarter 2021 earnings, with non-GAAP diluted net operating earnings per share (NOEPS) of $0.11, compared to $0.09 in the third quarter of 2020. The company's gas distribution segment saw an increase in operating revenues of approximately $8 million, while the electric segment's operating revenues decreased by about $2 million. NiSource's debt level as of June 30 was about $9.6 billion, with a weighted average maturity of approximately 14 years and a weighted average interest rate of approximately 3.7%. The company's capital investment estimate for 2021 has been narrowed to approximately $2 billion, and its financing plan remains consistent with its earnings growth and credit commitments. NiSource has updated its 2021 earnings guidance to target the top end of the range of a $1.32 to a $1.36 per share in non-GAAP diluted net operating earnings, and has initiated guidance for 2022 of a $1.42 to a $1.48. The company's long-term diluted NOEPS guidance through 2024 is now based on the expected top end of its 2021 guidance range, and it reaffirms 5% to 7% growth in 2023.
