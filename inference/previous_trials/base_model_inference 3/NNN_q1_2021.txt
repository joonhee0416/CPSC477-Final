 2021 Earnings Call Summary

The company announced an increase in its 2021 core FFO guidance by approximately 6% from a range of $2.55 to $2.62 per share to a range of $2.70 to $2.75 per share. The increase in guidance was driven by better-than-expected rent collections and the results from Q1. The company's portfolio of 3,161 freestanding single-tenant retail properties continued to perform well, with occupancy remaining above the long-term average. The company collected 97% of rents due for the first quarter and 98% of rents due for the month of April. The company's impressive collection results continue to compare favorably to other retail real estate companies. The company acquired 29 new properties in the quarter for just under $106 million, at an initial cash cap rate of 6.4% and with an average lease duration of 17.5 years. The company's focus remains on the long term as it continues to endeavor to give NNN the best opportunity to succeed in the coming years.
