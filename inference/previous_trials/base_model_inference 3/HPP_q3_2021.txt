 2021 Third Quarter Earnings Call Summary

Hudson Pacific Properties, Inc. (HPP) reported strong financial results for the third quarter of 2021, driven by the advantages of its focus on top-tier tech and media markets. The company's office and studio tenants continue to pay rent and repay deferred rent, with positive rent spreads and a stabilized lease percentage of over 92%. HPP successfully executed on multiple city-related acquisitions and announced plans for new studio projects, including the expansion of its studio platform in Los Angeles and the construction of a large-scale purpose-built facility in the UK. The company's combined expertise with Blackstone, across transactions, operations, and development, is second to none and positions it to deliver and operate next-generation studios that will attract premium productions for years to come. HPP's rent collections remained strong, with 99% for its portfolio overall and 100% for office and studio tenants. The company's third quarter NOI increased by 5.1% on a GAAP basis and 10.8% on a cash basis, with adjustments for one-time prior period property tax reimbursement. At the end of the third quarter, HPP had approximately $0.6 billion in liquidity with no material maturities until 2023 and an average loan term of 4.6 years. The company's guidance for 2021 full-year AFFO is in the range of $1.95 to $1.99 per diluted share, excluding specified items.
