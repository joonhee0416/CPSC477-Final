 2020 Q1 Earnings Summary

The company's net income on a GAAP basis was $22 million in Q1 2020, compared to $110 million in Q1 2019. Non-GAAP adjusted net income was $20 million in Q1 2020, compared to $2.30 per diluted share in Q1 2019. Adjusted EBITDA was $62 million in Q1 2020, compared to $181 million in the same period of 2019. The quarterly decrease was primarily driven by a 31% decrease in average net selling prices and a 13% decrease in sales volumes. The company's adjusted EBITDA margin was 27% in Q1 2020, compared to 48% in Q1 2019. Total revenues were approximately $227 million in Q1 2020, compared to $378 million in the same period last year. This decrease was primarily due to the decrease in average net selling prices and sales volumes in a weaker market environment than last year. The average net selling price per short ton decreased approximately 31% in Q1 2020 compared to the same period in 2019. The company expects the utilization of its NOLs will reduce its federal and state income tax liability to 0 until the NOLs are fully utilized or expired. The company withdrew its full year 2020 guidance due to the uncertainties regarding the duration of the COVID-19 pandemic and its overall impact on the global economy and the company's operations.
