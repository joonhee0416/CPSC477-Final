Havertys, a furniture retailer, reported strong results for the first quarter of 2021, with a 31.8% increase in delivered sales over the prior year quarter. The company's gross profit margin increased by 160 basis points, and its net income was $19.4 million. Havertys' inventory increased by $13.7 million over the December 31, 2020 balance, and its customer deposits increased by $18.5 million from the December 31, 2020 balance. The company expects its gross margins for 2021 to be between 56.5% and 57%.
