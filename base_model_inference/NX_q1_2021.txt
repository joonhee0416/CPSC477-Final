Quanex reported strong financial results for the first quarter of 2021, with revenue increasing by 17.1% compared to the same period in 2020. The increase was driven by increased demand for products across all product lines and operating segments. Net income for the quarter was $7.9 million, up from $10,000 in the same period in 2020. On an adjusted basis, net income increased to $9 million, and EBITDA increased by 55.4% to $24.3 million. The company's balance sheet is strong, with a liquidity position of solid, and its leverage ratio of net debt to last 12 months adjusted EBITDA is unchanged at 0.6 times. Quanex expects to generate low double-digit revenue growth this year while maintaining adjusted EBITDA margins in the low 12% range, despite increasing inflationary pressures.
