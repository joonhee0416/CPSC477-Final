2021 Earnings Call Summary for NiSource

NiSource is updating its guidance for 2021 to target the top end of the range of a $1.32 to a $1.36 per share in non-GAAP diluted net operating earnings or NOEPS. The company is also initiating guidance for 2022 of a $1.42 to a $1.48, which is consistent with its 5% to 7% near-term growth commitment. NiSource's long-term diluted NOEPS guidance of 7% to 9% through 2024 is now based on the expected top end of its 2021 guidance range, and the company reaffirms 5% to 7% growth in 2023. The company is also investing in renewable energy, with plans to retire remaining coal-fired generation between 2026 and 2028 and reduce greenhouse gas emissions 90% by 2030. NiSource is also pursuing regulatory approvals for its gas rate cases in Ohio, Indiana, and Kentucky, and has filed a gas rate case in Maryland. The company has also achieved non-GAAP diluted NOEPS of $0.11 in the third quarter of 2021 versus $0.09 in 2020.
