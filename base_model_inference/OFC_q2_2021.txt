2021 Earnings Call Summary

The company's unique investment strategy of clustering assets adjacent to priority Department of Defense missions continues to generate strong high-quality earnings. The second quarter FFO per share as adjusted for comparability of $0.58 exceeded the high end of guidance by $0.01, driven primarily by same property results. NOI from real estate operations in the quarter was up 8%, and AFFO increased 17% from a year ago. The company has completed total leasing of 1.7 million square feet, which includes 815,000 square feet of renewals, 205,000 square feet of vacancy leasing, and 641,000 square feet of development leasing. The company expects to achieve its 1 million square foot growth for the year. The company has a strong set of development and leasing opportunities before it and the balance sheet and access to capital to seize upon them. The company's key performance metrics such as vacancy leasing and development leasing are tracking at or above plan. The company's strategy of concentrating investments adjacent to priority Department of Defense missions and creating value through low risk developments at these locations is delivering FFO growth and lowering the cost of capital. The company has a highly durable operating portfolio, strong balance sheet, and a reliable, low-risk development program that combined to create the very visible growth it is delivering.
