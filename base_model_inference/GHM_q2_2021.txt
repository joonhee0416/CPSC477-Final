Graham Corporation reported its fiscal 2021 second quarter results, with sales increasing to $28 million from $21.6 million in the same period last year. The company's defense market accounted for $9.4 million of the quarter's revenue, with a quick turn material order making up approximately half of the revenue. The company's gross profit increased to $7.7 million, with gross margins of 27.5%, and EBITDA margins of 14.2%. The company's net income for the quarter was $2.7 million, up from $1.2 million in the same period last year. For the first half of the year, sales were $44.7 million, with gross profit of $9.3 million and net income of $900,000. The company's backlog improved to $114.9 million, with 60% of the backlog planned to convert during the next four quarters. The company is updating its revenue guidance for the fiscal year, with a range of $93 million to $97 million, and expects to see a strong demand for its products in the second half of the year.
