Net sales rose 29.1% in Q3, driven by expanded product selection, faster delivery, and enhanced customer experiences. The company has invested heavily in infrastructure, talent, and technology to support long-term growth. New partnerships with Samsung, LG, and other brands have contributed to sales growth. To improve fulfillment, it has expanded its network with new shipping hubs in Nevada and Pennsylvania. A new customer care facility in Florida will enhance customer support. Financially, the company reported a net loss of $105.4 million, primarily due to increased SG&A expenses. However, it has a strong cash position of over $1.4 billion and no outstanding debt. The company emphasizes long-term revenue growth and scale as the foundation for future profitability. It continues to invest in e-commerce, blockchain, NFTs, and Web 3.0 gaming to explore future opportunities.
