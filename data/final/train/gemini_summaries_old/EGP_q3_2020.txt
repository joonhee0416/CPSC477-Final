EastGroup Properties reported strong third quarter results, with funds from operations (FFO) per share exceeding guidance and rising by 6.3% year-over-year. This marks 30 consecutive quarters of higher FFO per share. Despite ongoing uncertainty, the company raised its FFO guidance to $5.35 per share, which exceeds pre-pandemic expectations. The company's portfolio remains resilient, with occupancy averaging 96.6% and rent collections at 97.6% for October to date. EastGroup is responding to market strength by restarting development, with plans for some fourth quarter starts and additional starts in the first quarter of 2021. The company has also been working on new land sites and park expansion. Key financial metrics remain strong, with a low debt-to-total market capitalization ratio of 19% and ample liquidity. The company's dividend was increased by 5.3% to $0.79 per share, representing a yield of 4.7% based on the current share price. EastGroup remains optimistic about the future, citing accelerating e-commerce and last-mile delivery trends, as well as the continued need for last-mile distribution space in growing Sun Belt markets.
