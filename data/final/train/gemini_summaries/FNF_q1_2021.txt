FNF reported strong first-quarter results, with revenue growth of 62.6% to $3.1 billion. The Title segment drove growth, with record adjusted pre-tax earnings of $512 million and a 19.9% margin. Title orders opened averaged 12,600 per day, driven by a surge in purchase activity. F&G's annuity sales reached record levels, with fixed indexed annuity sales up 11% from the previous quarter. Asset growth and disciplined spread management contributed to strong earnings, while credit rating upgrades have enabled expansion into institutional products. The company remains committed to long-term value creation through capital allocation and business growth.
