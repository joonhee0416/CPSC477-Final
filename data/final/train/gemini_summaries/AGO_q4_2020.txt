Despite the challenges of the COVID-19 pandemic, Assured Guaranty delivered strong results in 2020, with record highs for adjusted book value per share ($114.87) and adjusted operating shareholder's equity per share ($78.49). The company's new business production exceeded $389 million in direct PVP, driven by strong performance in the U.S. municipal bond insurance sector, where the company guaranteed over $21 billion of primary and secondary market transactions. Assured Guaranty also repurchased 16.2 million shares, with $515 million returned to shareholders through repurchases and dividends. In the asset management segment, the company saw a 62% increase in fee-earning AUM to $12.9 billion. For 2021, Assured Guaranty is optimistic about its financial strength and liquidity and believes it will continue to find opportunities to assist issuers with financing costs.
