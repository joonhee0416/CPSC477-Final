Winnebago Industries reported strong financial performance in Q2, with record consolidated revenues of $839.9 million, a 34% increase over Q2 2020. Gross margin expanded by 590 basis points to 18.6%, driven by favorable pricing, productivity initiatives, and segment mix. Adjusted EBITDA margin increased by 570 basis points to 12.9%, resulting in record adjusted EPS of $2.12, a 216% increase year-over-year. The company's Towable segment saw revenue growth of 55%, while the Motorhome segment experienced a 17.5% increase. Winnebago Industries is investing in capacity expansion projects to meet strong consumer demand, which remains elevated despite the pandemic. The company also continues to prioritize corporate responsibility initiatives, including funding a program to create more equitable outdoor spaces for women and outdoor enthusiasts of color.
