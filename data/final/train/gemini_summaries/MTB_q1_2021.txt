M&T Bank reported $3.33 in diluted GAAP earnings per common share for Q1 2021, a decrease from $3.52 in Q4 2020 but an increase from $1.93 in Q1 of 2020. The bank's net income was $447 million, down from $471 million in Q4 2020, but higher than the $269 million reported for the year-ago quarter. M&T's efficiency ratio was 60.3% in the recent quarter. The allowance for credit losses declined to $1.6 billion from $1.7 billion at the end of 2020, indicating an improving economic outlook. The bank's common equity Tier 1 ratio remained strong at an estimated 10.4%. Management noted that the merger with People's United Financial is pending and that the bank does not plan to engage in stock repurchase activity during this period.
