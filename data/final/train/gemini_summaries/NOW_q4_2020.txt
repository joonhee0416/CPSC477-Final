In the fourth quarter of 2020, ServiceNow exceeded expectations with strong financial performance. Subscription revenues reached $1.184 billion, reflecting a 32% year-over-year growth, while subscription billings surged by 41% to $1.828 billion. The company's strong momentum continued with a best-in-class 99% renewal rate and a growing number of customers with annual payments exceeding $1 million. Key wins in Q4 included multi-year agreements with AT&T, a major UK bank, and PayPal, showcasing the company's ability to deliver comprehensive solutions across industries. ServiceNow's innovative Now Platform, which offers one platform, one data model, and one architecture, is driving the company's success as it meets the growing demand for digital transformation and workflow optimization.
