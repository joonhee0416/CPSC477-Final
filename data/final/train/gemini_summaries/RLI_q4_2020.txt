RLI Corporation delivered a strong finish to 2020 with the fourth quarter operating earnings of $0.75 per share, driven by favorable reserve developments and improving current year casualty results. The company achieved a 10% top line growth and posted an 88 combined ratio. Despite elevated catastrophe losses, the 25th consecutive year of underwriting profit was sustained with a 92 combined ratio. RLI's gross premiums increased by 7%, while GAAP equity grew to $1.1 billion. The company also announced its CEO succession plan, with Craig Kliethermes assuming the role in 2022. Looking forward, RLI remains optimistic about its market position, citing favorable trends such as retrenchment of competitors and rate increases. The company's diverse portfolio of products and underwriting discipline position it to capitalize on opportunities in the coming year.
