Despite challenges presented by the COVID-19 pandemic, UScellular reported a solid financial performance in 2020. With maintained low churn rates, growth in postpaid net adds, increased service revenue, and decreased cash expenses, the company saw an increase in adjusted EBITDA of 5% year-over-year. For 2021, UScellular expects continued growth, aiming to drive revenue through regional expansion, enhanced digital capabilities, and a focus on prepaid and B2B segments. The company plans to invest in network modernization, 5G deployment, and fixed-wireless access using millimeter wave spectrum. To optimize return on capital, UScellular will prioritize revenue growth, expense discipline, and capital optimization. The company also emphasizes network performance and plans to expand its 5G footprint and improve customer experience.
