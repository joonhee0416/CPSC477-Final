MasTec reported strong financial results for the fourth quarter and full year 2020. Revenue for the fourth quarter was $1.6 billion, with adjusted EBITDA of $262 million and adjusted earnings per share of $1.75. Full-year 2020 revenue was $6.3 billion, adjusted EBITDA was $810 million, and adjusted earnings per share were $5.11. Notably, non-oil and gas revenues and EBITDA grew significantly in 2020, and this trend is expected to continue in 2021 with an estimated 20% revenue growth and 45% EBITDA growth. The company experienced strong performance in its Communications and Clean Energy segments, and expects double-digit revenue growth in all segments in 2021. MasTec has a record backlog of $7.9 billion and remains active in pursuing acquisitions to further drive growth. The guidance for 2021 reflects a strong 24% revenue growth, with revenues and EBITDA expected to reach record levels. The company also highlighted its commitment to clean energy investments and the opportunities arising from recent events in Texas.
