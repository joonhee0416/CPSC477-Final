ADM's third-quarter earnings report showed a 14.3% year-over-year increase, with adjusted earnings per share rising from $0.77 to $0.89, exceeding expectations. Adjusted segment operating profit was up 11% at $849 million, and the trailing four-quarter adjusted ROIC reached 8.3%. The Nutrition segment continued its growth trajectory, reporting a fifth consecutive quarter of 20-plus percent year-over-year operating profit growth. Ag Services and Oilseeds saw improved margins, while Carbohydrate Solutions benefited from strong risk management and cost optimization. The company's balance sheet remains strong, with a net debt to total capital ratio of 27% and a positive tax rate outlook. ADM's continued investment in readiness initiatives unlocked significant benefits, including an anticipated $1.3 billion in run rate benefits by year-end. The company's focus on sustainability, innovation, and customer-centricity positions it well for continued growth and leadership in the nutrition sector.
