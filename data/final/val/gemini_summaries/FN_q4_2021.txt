Fabrinet concluded a strong fiscal year with record revenue of $509.6 million in Q4, exceeding guidance and representing a 26% year-over-year increase. The company also achieved a record non-GAAP operating margin of 9.9%, resulting in non-GAAP earnings per share of $1.31. For the full year, revenue reached a record $1.88 billion, with non-GAAP earnings per share increasing 25% to $4.67. Optical communications revenue remained strong, driven by telecom demand, while non-optical communications revenue also set new highs. Looking ahead to Q1 of fiscal 2022, Fabrinet anticipates revenue in the range of $510 million to $530 million, with non-GAAP net income per diluted share between $1.29 and $1.36. Despite supply constraints, the company remains optimistic about demand trends and has taken measures to ensure business continuity amid COVID-19 challenges.
