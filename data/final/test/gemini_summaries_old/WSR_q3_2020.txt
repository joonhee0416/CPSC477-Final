Whitestone REIT successfully navigated the challenges of COVID-19 with strong shopping center sector-leading results, including 90% cash collections and sector-leading rental collections throughout the pandemic. The company's business model, centered around properties in high-growth markets and targeting e-commerce-resistant tenants, has proven resilient. Whitestone attributes its success to its strategic customer focus, operating with a disciplined capital allocation philosophy that has guided its growth since 2010. Despite the economic headwinds, the company has improved its dividend payout ratio, strengthened its balance sheet, and achieved positive third-quarter operating results. Whitestone remains focused on its long-term goals of improving debt leverage, scaling G&A costs, and continuing to grow its portfolio through redevelopment, development, and acquisitions in its existing and targeted markets. The company is confident in its position to withstand current headwinds and thrive in the future.
