Clean Harbors concluded 2019 with a strong Q4, driven by growth in environmental services and the SK branch business. Revenue increased 1% in the quarter, resulting in an 8% growth in adjusted EBITDA and a 100-basis-point margin improvement. For the full year, adjusted EBITDA grew 10% and record adjusted free cash flow of $208.5 million was generated. In 2020, the company anticipates profitable growth with a focus on leveraging its facilities and service locations, pricing initiatives, and project volume increases. Clean Harbors also plans to capitalize on the shifting market conditions brought on by IMO 2020 and pursue emerging growth opportunities, such as PFAS and sustainability offerings. The company reported adjusted EBITDA guidance of $545 million to $585 million for 2020, representing a 5% increase from 2019. Adjusted free cash flow is projected to be in the range of $210 million to $240 million.
