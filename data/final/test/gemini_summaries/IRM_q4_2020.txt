Iron Mountain witnessed challenges from COVID-19 in 2020 but remained resilient, outperforming internal expectations in the past three quarters. They shifted their focus to growth in the face of continued headwinds, resulting in optimistic guidance for 2021. Key financial performance indicators showed strength, with total organic revenue up 2.4% and EBITDA margin expansion of 110 basis points. Looking ahead, they anticipate organic revenue growth of 2% to 6%, the highest in the past decade. Iron Mountain's strategic growth initiatives prioritize data center expansion, including a new venture in India's rapidly growing market. They are also focused on high-potential areas such as digital solutions, consumer storage, and secure IT asset disposition. The company remains committed to environmental sustainability, aiming for 100% carbon neutrality well before 2050 despite data center growth. With a strong pipeline, improved visibility, and a highly capable team, Iron Mountain is well-positioned to execute its strategy and drive growth in 2021.
