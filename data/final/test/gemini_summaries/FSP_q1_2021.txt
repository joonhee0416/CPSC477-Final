Franklin Street Properties reported Q1 FFO of $0.17 per share. The company remains committed to its Sunbelt and Mountain West office focus. As of March 31, 2021, total unsecured debt outstanding was $947.5 million, including $27.5 million drawn on their line of credit. The company has no debt maturities until November 2021, when $155 million of term loans will become due. FSP has approximately 800,000 square feet of prospective tenants interested in leasing space, compared to 300,000 square feet last quarter. FSP has engaged with existing tenants for approximately 250,000 square feet of renewals and expansions. Potential aggregate net absorption is approximately 600,000 square feet over the next 6-12 months, the highest since Q1 2020. The company is working towards its disposition goal of $350 million to $450 million for 2021 to pay down debt and gain financial flexibility. FSP is seeing positive interest in office investment from selective investors despite the decline in office sales nationally.
