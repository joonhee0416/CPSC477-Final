In Q4 2020, TrueBlue posted positive net income and adjusted net income despite a 12% decline in revenue to $519 million. The company's revenue trends have improved each quarter since Q2 2020. PeopleReady, TrueBlue's largest segment, experienced an 18% revenue decline, while PeopleManagement saw a 5% growth. PeopleScout, impacted by travel and leisure clients, faced a 24% decline. TrueBlue implemented cost-cutting measures, reducing SG&A by 22%. The company's digital strategy, particularly the JobStack app, has driven efficiency and client user growth. TrueBlue expects additional gross margin pressure in Q1 but sees potential for expansion later in 2021. The company ended the year with a strong balance sheet, cash flow from operations of $153 million, and no outstanding debt.
