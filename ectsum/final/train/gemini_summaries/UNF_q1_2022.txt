UniFirst reported solid Q1 results, with consolidated revenues increasing by 8.8% to $486.2 million. The core laundry operations' adjusted operating margin was approximately 10%, despite facing cost pressures related to raw materials, supply chain disruptions, labor, and energy. The company witnessed positive wearer additions, indicating customer base growth and recovery. Specialty garments revenue grew by 3.5%, while the first aid segment experienced a revenue increase of 14.8%. UniFirst's strategic initiatives, including CRM implementation, brand investments, and an ERP system, incurred expenses that impacted adjusted profitability. Despite these challenges, the company maintains a strong balance sheet with no long-term debt and cash equivalents totaling $478.1 million. Looking ahead, UniFirst expects fiscal 2022 revenues to range from $1.94 billion to $1.955 billion, with diluted EPS between $5.50 and $5.80. Adjusted diluted EPS is projected to be between $7.00 and $7.30.
