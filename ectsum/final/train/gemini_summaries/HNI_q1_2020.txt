Despite the pandemic's impact, HNI Corporation's Q1 results demonstrate strong operation and strategic focus. Non-GAAP net income per diluted share increased by 950% to $0.21, with a 2.2% decline in sales to $468.7 million. The company has implemented cost-saving measures to navigate the downturn, including temporary salary reductions for all exempt salaried employees and executives, furloughs, suspension of share repurchase activity, and a reduction in capital expenditure budget. Analysis indicates the ability to manage deleverage and generate free cash flow above current dividend levels with zero cash earnings. HNI is focused on customer-centricity, simplifying the buying process, and leveraging lean heritage for long-term improvement.
