FIS delivered exceptional financial results in Q2, with revenue surging 17% year-over-year to a record $3.5 billion, driven by strong demand for its solutions. Adjusted earnings per share grew 40%, reflecting improved operational efficiency. Cross-selling initiatives yielded impressive results, increasing the revenue synergy run rate by 50% to $450 million. This performance led to an increase in backlog to over $22 billion. Based on the strong Q2 results, the company raised its full-year guidance, now anticipating revenue of $13.9 billion to $14 billion and adjusted EBITDA of $6.125 billion to $6.2 billion. Merchant growth is projected to approach 20%, with e-commerce revenue expanding 31%. FIS remains committed to its pivot to growth strategy, investing in innovation and expanding its sales force to drive continued acceleration in revenue and earnings per share.
