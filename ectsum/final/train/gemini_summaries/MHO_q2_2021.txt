EMI Homes reported impressive second-quarter results, highlighted by a 97% increase in net income and a 35% surge in revenue. Gross margins improved by 320 basis points, leading to a significant increase in pre-tax income from 10% to 14.7%. Despite operating with fewer communities, new contracts rose by 24%, driven by strong demand for new homes. Mortgage and title operations also performed exceptionally, with pre-tax income increasing by 66%. The company remains well-positioned for growth with an expected expansion of communities in the second half of 2022. Strong financial metrics, including a 27% return on equity and a solid balance sheet, provide confidence in the company's future prospects.
