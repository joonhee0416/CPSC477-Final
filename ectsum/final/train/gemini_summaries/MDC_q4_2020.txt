MDC delivered strong Q4 2020 results, with diluted earnings per share of $2.19, a 54% increase year-over-year. Home sales revenues increased 10%, while home sales gross margin expanded 350 basis points to 22%. Net new orders increased 92%, resulting in a record backlog value of $3.26 billion, up 87% year-over-year. The company's financial position is strong, with increased liquidity and a low cost of capital. MDC plans to expand into new markets, including Boise, Idaho, and targets a 10% increase in active subdivisions in 2021. Despite potential risks, the company remains optimistic about the housing market and expects demand to continue after the pandemic subsides. MDC remains committed to shareholder rewards, having declared a $0.40 per share cash dividend and an 8% stock dividend.
