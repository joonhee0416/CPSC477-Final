JBT reported strong earnings amidst supply chain disruptions and inflationary pressures. FoodTech led the growth with a 22% year-over-year revenue increase and a $386 million record order intake. FoodTech margins were in line with guidance, while AeroTech margins exceeded expectations. Adjusted earnings per share came in at $0.90, exceeding expectations, and free cash flow reached $78 million. JBT raised its full-year revenue growth guidance for FoodTech to 9%-11% but lowered margin guidance due to inflationary and operational challenges. The company expects a sequential ramp in performance over the next three quarters and is looking to expand its balance sheet to support increased sales. M&A opportunities are being considered to enhance FoodTech's position and expand its reach.
