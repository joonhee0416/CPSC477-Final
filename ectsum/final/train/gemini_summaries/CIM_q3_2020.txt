Chimera Investment's Q3 results saw a 12% increase in book value to $11.91 per share, indicating a strong financial position. The company generated $0.33 in core earnings per share and paid a $0.30 dividend, resulting in a near-15% economic return. Driven by a robust housing market and record-low interest rates, demand for single-family homes is booming. Chimera has capitalized on this trend, with nearly 90% of its portfolio allocated to mortgage credit. The company has also been successful in securitizing loans, completing three such transactions this quarter. Chimera's investment portfolio remains well-positioned, with a strong housing market and Ample liquidity to acquire new loan pools.
