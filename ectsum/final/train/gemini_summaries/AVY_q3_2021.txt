Despite supply chain challenges and inflationary pressures, Avery Dennison achieved strong top-line growth in its Label and Graphic Materials and Retail Branding and Information Solutions segments. Intelligent Label sales rose 15% in the quarter, with 30% expected organic growth for the year. The Industrial and Healthcare Materials segment rebounded with double-digit growth in ASEAN and Latin America. Despite inflationary pressures, margins remained strong, particularly in Retail Branding and Information Solutions, which achieved a 13.8% adjusted operating margin. The company raised its 2021 adjusted earnings per share guidance to between $8.80 and $8.95, with 15% organic sales growth expected for the year. Key challenges include raw material shortages and COVID-19 restrictions, leading to increased inflation and supply chain constraints.
