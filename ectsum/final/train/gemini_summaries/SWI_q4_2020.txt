SolarWinds reported strong financial results for 2020, achieving $1 billion in non-GAAP revenues and a 48% adjusted EBITDA margin. Subscription revenue grew by 22%, and the customer base expanded to over 25,000 MSP partners servicing 500,000 small and medium businesses. Despite the cyberattack, the company sustained strong maintenance renewal rates and expects license revenue growth to improve throughout 2021. The company is investing in security-related initiatives, with incremental expenses estimated at $20-25 million in 2020-2021. SolarWinds also plans to spin off its MSP business in the second quarter of 2021 and is exploring additional inorganic investments to enhance its portfolio and market opportunities.
