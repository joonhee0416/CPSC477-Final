During the first quarter, Sensient Technologies reported 4% consolidated adjusted local currency revenue growth, with the Flavors & Extracts Group and Asia Pacific Group driving growth. The Flavors & Extracts Group had strong performance, with 9% adjusted local currency revenue growth and 21% adjusted local currency profit growth. The company's debt-to-EBITDA ratio has improved from 2.9 to 2.4, and its balance sheet remains strong. Despite supply chain challenges, Sensient Technologies has continued to provide robust customer service and has mitigated raw material shortages and cost increases. The company acquired a natural ingredients production facility, which is expected to contribute to growth later this year. Adjusted local currency EBITDA increased by approximately 2% for the quarter, and Sensient Technologies reconfirmed its previously issued 2021 guidance.
