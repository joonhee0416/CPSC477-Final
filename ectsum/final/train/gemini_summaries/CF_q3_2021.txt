CF Industries reported strong financial results driven by favorable industry conditions, with nitrogen prices reaching their highest levels in over a decade and strong demand. The company's trailing 12-month net cash from operations reached $1.7 billion, while free cash flow stood at $1 billion. Looking ahead, the company expects adjusted EBITDA for 2021 to range between $2.2 billion and $2.4 billion. The positive industry conditions are expected to continue for an extended period, supporting CF Industries' ability to generate significant free cash. The company remains focused on disciplined investments and returning excess capital to shareholders through dividends and share repurchases, with a newly authorized $1.5 billion share repurchase program. CF Industries is also investing in clean energy initiatives, including producing over one million tons of blue ammonia annually through projects at Donaldsonville and Yard of the City.
