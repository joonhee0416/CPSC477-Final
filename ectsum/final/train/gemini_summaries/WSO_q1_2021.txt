Watsco achieved record first-quarter results, with earnings per share surging by 93% to $1.39. Growth was driven by strong sales across all product categories and geographies, as well as higher selling margins and improved efficiencies. Notably, residential HVAC equipment sales increased by 18% in the U.S. over the last 12 months, indicating significant market share gains. Watsco anticipates a strong 2021 and remains optimistic in its outlook. The company's industry-leading technology continues to gain adoption, leading to customer acquisition and increased market share. Watsco recently closed the acquisition of Temperature Equipment Corporation, adding 32 locations and approximately $300 million in revenue. Furthermore, Watsco raised its dividend by 10% in April 2021, marking its 47th consecutive year of dividend payments and 19th dividend increase in the past 20 years.
