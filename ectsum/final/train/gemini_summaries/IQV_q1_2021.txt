IQVIA reported strong first-quarter results, with revenue growing 24% on a reported basis and 21% at constant currency, exceeding guidance. Adjusted EBITDA increased by 32%, and adjusted diluted EPS grew by 45%, both surpassing expectations. The company cited strong operational performance and pent-up demand in the industry as key factors contributing to its success. IQVIA's commercial technology presence continues to expand, with new deployments of its next best action solution and a growing user base for its eCOA technology platform. In R&DS, the company achieved a net book-to-bill ratio of 1.41, indicating strong bookings momentum. As a result of continued operational momentum and an improved outlook, IQVIA has once again raised its guidance for the full year, projecting revenue growth of 16.2% to 18.8% and adjusted diluted EPS growth of 32.4% to 36.3%.
