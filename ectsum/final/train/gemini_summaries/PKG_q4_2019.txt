In Q4 2019, PCA's net income was $136 million ($1.43 per share), excluding special items of $26 million. Compared to Q4 2018, earnings decreased by $0.46 per share due to factors such as lower prices, higher operating costs, and increased non-operating pension and depreciation expenses. The Packaging segment reported lower prices and mix, but higher volumes and benefits from the new Richland plant. For the full year 2019, PCA's net income was $726 million ($7.65 per share), excluding special items, with a Packaging segment EBITDA margin of 22.1%. In 2020, PCA expects lower Q1 earnings of $1.20 per share due to scheduled mill outages and other factors, such as lower prices, higher costs, and increased depreciation expenses.
