FactSet reported strong third-quarter results, highlighted by a 5.8% organic ASV plus professional services growth, driven by increased demand for its content and technology offerings. The growth was particularly strong with wealth managers, banking clients, and asset owners. Despite elevated expenses in compensation and technology, FactSet managed to increase its full-year organic ASV plus professional services guidance range to $85-95 million. The company's multiyear investment plan, including cloud migration and content expansion, continues to strengthen its product portfolio and drive client demand. Notably, client retention remained high, with ASV retention exceeding 95% and client retention improving to 91%. FactSet also repurchased 178,000 shares in the third quarter and increased its quarterly dividend by 6.5%. The company remains confident in executing on its pipeline and growing its market share.
