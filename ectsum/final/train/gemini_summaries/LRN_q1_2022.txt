Despite the ongoing challenges posed by the pandemic, supply chain disruptions, and labor shortages, Stride witnessed a surge in demand for educational options, particularly for virtual learning. This increased demand stems from heightened awareness among parents about the need for alternative education models. Stride observed a surge in website traffic, peaking beyond pandemic levels, and a significant increase in conversion rates as families became more comfortable with virtual education. Revenue for the fiscal year is projected to reach $1.56 billion to $1.6 billion, with adjusted operating income expected to range from $165 million to $180 million. Stride's ongoing commitment to improving the customer experience and simplifying the enrollment process is expected to lead to further improvements in the future.
