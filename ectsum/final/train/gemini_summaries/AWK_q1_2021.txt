In the first quarter of 2021, American Water reported earnings per share of $0.73, a 7.4% increase from $0.68 in the same quarter of 2020. The company's regulated business segment saw a $0.06 per share increase, driven by infrastructure investments, acquisitions, and organic growth, while the market-based business experienced a $0.03 per share decrease due to weather-related events. Despite this, the company remains confident in its long-term earnings per share growth rate of 7% to 10%, supported by $123 million in annualized new revenues from rate adjustments and infrastructure surcharges. American Water also continues to deliver on its commitment to dividend growth, increasing its quarterly cash dividend payment from $0.55 to $0.6025 per share, representing a 10% compound annual growth rate over the past five years. The company's strong regulatory execution and positive market-based business results enable it to consistently deliver on its earnings commitment, providing excellent value for shareholders.
