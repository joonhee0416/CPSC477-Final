Boston Beer Company reported strong second-quarter growth despite COVID-19 challenges, with depletions increasing by 46% driven by Truly Hard Seltzer and Twisted Tea. Truly's growth accelerated, gaining market share even amid increased competition. Samuel Adams and Angry Orchard experienced declines due to on-premise closures, but showed growth in off-premise channels. The company expects continued growth for Truly, Twisted Tea, and Dogfish Head brands, and anticipates full-year depletions between 27% and 35%, with 1% to 2% attributed to Dogfish Head. Gross margins are projected between 46% and 48% for the full year, with advertising and promotional expenses increasing by $70 million to $80 million. Boston Beer remains committed to safety and supporting bar and restaurant workers through its Samuel Adams Restaurants Strong Fund.
