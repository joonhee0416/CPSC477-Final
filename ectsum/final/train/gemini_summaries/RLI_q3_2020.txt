Despite severe hurricanes impacting the quarter, RLI Corporation achieved a 9% top line growth with a 99.5 combined ratio, resulting in $0.42 earnings per share. Strong pricing momentum contributed to the growth, particularly in casualty (11%), property (8%), and surety (1%). Hurricane losses totaled $39 million, mainly impacting property and casualty segments. The company also recorded $4 million in COVID-19-related losses, resulting in $15 million in year-to-date reserves. Despite the challenges, RLI remains optimistic about market conditions, with rate increases outpacing loss trends. The company emphasizes its differentiation through in-person customer connections and investments in sales and technology. Overall, the quarter displayed resilience and the company's competitive position, driven by disciplined underwriting and a focus on financially secure clients.
