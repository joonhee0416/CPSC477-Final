In the second quarter of 2021, the company reported strong growth of 25% and an impressive combined ratio of 84.8. The positive results were driven by underwriting profits and favorable reserve developments. All three segments experienced growth, with Property leading with a 33% increase. Casualty reported a strong 83 combined ratio, driven by significant reserve development and rate increases in excess liability and auto markets. Surety, despite adverse loss development in the energy sector, achieved a 96 combined ratio through disciplined underwriting and reinsurance protection. Notably, the company's diversified portfolio and underwriting discipline allow it to navigate market conditions while maintaining underwriting margins and shareholder value. Overall, the second quarter and first half of the year were highly successful, with a 11% increase in book value per share.
