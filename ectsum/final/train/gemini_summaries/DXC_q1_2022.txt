DXC Technology's Q1 2022 earnings call highlights continued progress in its transformation journey. Revenue reached $4.14 billion, exceeding estimates, with adjusted EBIT margin improving to 8%. Non-GAAP earnings per share surged 300% to $0.84. The company's focus on customer engagement and cost optimization contributed to these positive results. Book-to-bill ratio maintained above 1.0 for the fifth straight quarter, indicating strong market demand. DXC plans to expand adjusted EBIT margins and deliver a consistent book-to-bill over 1.0 in Q2. Moreover, the company emphasizes debt reduction, balance sheet strengthening, improved cash flow, and strategic capital deployment to enhance its financial foundation.
