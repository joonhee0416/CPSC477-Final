National Retail Properties reported another strong quarter with increased acquisition volume, high occupancy (98.6%), and impressive rent collections (99%). The company's 2021 core FFO per share guidance was raised to $2.80-$2.84, representing a 2% to 2.5% increase over 2019 levels. For 2022, they project further growth with a core FFO per share guidance of $2.90-$2.97. Acquisitions are expected to return to pre-pandemic levels with guidance in the range of $550-$650 million. National Retail Properties maintains a strong balance sheet, highlighted by a 3% interest-only 30-year debt issuance in September, $200 million in cash on hand, a zero balance on their credit line, and a low weighted average debt duration of almost 15 years.
