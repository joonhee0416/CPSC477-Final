Pitney Bowes's Presort business excelled in revenue and profitability due to effective cost management and technology advancements. Despite supply constraints, SendTech maintained performance with increased equipment sales and a shift towards subscription revenue. Global Ecommerce experienced improved gross margins and enhanced customer service. The company anticipates potential peak season challenges due to supply chain disruptions and has implemented pricing adjustments to mitigate increased transportation and labor costs. Despite these challenges, Pitney Bowes reaffirms its revenue growth target of low to mid-single-digit percentage growth for the year and adjusted earnings per share between $0.35 and $0.42. The company remains optimistic about its financial position and is well-equipped to achieve its long-term goals.
