Kite Realty Group (KRG) reported strong Q2 results, including a nearly doubled leasing volume and a 10.1% increase in same-property NOI. The company's anchor acceleration program has signed seven leases for a cumulative yield of over 36%. Despite the pandemic, KRG maintains a strong financial position with a net debt-to-EBITDA ratio of 6.4 times and $620 million in liquidity. KRG is optimistic about the merger with RPAI, which is expected to create a best-in-class platform. The merger transaction is ongoing, and no additional details were provided. KRG raised its 2021 adjusted FFO guidance to $1.29-$1.35 per share, driven by continued operational outperformance.
