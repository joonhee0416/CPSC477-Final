Jabil delivered strong Q3 results, surpassing expectations with $7.2 billion in revenue and a core operating margin of 3.8%. Driven by diverse and resilient end markets, the company saw balanced contributions across its commercial portfolio. Looking ahead, Jabil anticipates core earnings per share of approximately $5.50 for FY '21 on revenue of $29.5 billion. The company maintained its core operating margin outlook of 4.2% and increased its free cash flow outlook to $630 million. Moreover, Jabil has a strong balance sheet with $5 billion in available liquidity. For Q4, the company expects revenue between $7.3 billion to $7.9 billion, a 4% year-over-year increase. Core operating income is estimated to range from $280 million to $340 million, resulting in core diluted earnings per share of $1.25 to $1.45. The long-term secular trends in healthcare, automotive, and other end markets are expected to continue driving sustainable growth in FY '21 and beyond.
