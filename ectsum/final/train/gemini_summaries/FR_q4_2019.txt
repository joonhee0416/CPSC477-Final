First Industrial Realty Trust reported a strong fourth quarter, with occupancy at 97.6% and full-year cash rental rate growth of 13.9%. The company developed high-quality facilities with strong margins and expanded its pipeline. For 2020, the company anticipates cash rental rate growth of 10-14% and NAREIT FFO per share of $1.77-$1.87. It has high occupancy and a favorable market environment with low vacancy rates and strong demand. The company's guidance factors in the assumption that Pier 1 Imports will continue to occupy its property and generates $2.5 million in FFO.
