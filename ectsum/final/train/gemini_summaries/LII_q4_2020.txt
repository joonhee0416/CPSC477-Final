Reporting a solid fourth quarter, the company exceeded its revenue target with $914 million, a 3% increase year-over-year. Residential segment led the growth with an 18% profit increase and a 130 basis point margin expansion. Despite a revenue dip in Commercial, total segment profit and margin achieved new highs for the fourth quarter. Adjusted earnings per share from continuing operations surged 18% to $2.89. The company maintains its 2021 guidance, projecting 48% revenue growth, with earnings per share ranging from $10.55 to $11.15. Plans for 2021 include investments in R&D and IT, $135 million in capital expenditures, and $400 million in stock repurchases.
