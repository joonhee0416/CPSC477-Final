Vector Group experienced a rise in both revenue and net income in Q4 2020, with total revenue rising to $554.6 million from $439.6 million in the previous year, and net income increasing from $10.7 million to $32.3 million. Douglas Elliman, a subsidiary of Vector Group, also showed resilience in the second half of 2020, with revenue of $267.5 million, net income of $14 million, and adjusted EBITDA of $16.7 million in Q4. Vector Group noted strong results for its tobacco business, with increased market share, driven by the success of its Eagle 20s brand, and the introduction of the Montego brand. The company's cash reserves remain robust, with $353 million in cash and cash equivalents as of December 31, 2020. Vector Group has consistently paid quarterly cash dividends and expects to continue this trend in the future.
