The company experienced a strong Q2, resulting in an increased FFO guidance of $1.93 per share, considering development pipeline expansion and robust operating results. Despite a slight decrease in occupancy, an increase in the straight-line overall rental rates by 29.5% was observed, with new leasing seeing a 34.4% increase. The company's leasing activity was strong, with 1.2 million square feet of development leases secured in Q2 and Q3. Additionally, favorable financing arrangements were made, with pricing reductions on both line of credit and term loan extensions, resulting in a weighted average interest rate of 3.4% on unsecured notes, term loans, and secured financings. Furthermore, the company has a robust pipeline of future projects, setting them up for continued growth and shareholder value creation.
