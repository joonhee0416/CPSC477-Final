O-I Glass reported strong second quarter results, exceeding guidance due to favorable shipment levels and ongoing operating performance. Adjusted earnings per share reached $0.54, reflecting an 18% increase in shipments and 27% production rebound compared to the prior year impacted by the pandemic. The company's margin expansion initiatives have surpassed expectations, with benefits totaling $40 million in the first half of 2021 and exceeding the original $50 million target for the year. O-I remains optimistic about its business outlook, with improved earnings and cash flow guidance for 2021. The company anticipates third quarter adjusted earnings of $0.47 to $0.52 and full year earnings between $1.65 to $1.75 per share. This reflects higher expected shipment levels, increased 4% to 5% compared to 2020, and continued solid operating performance.
