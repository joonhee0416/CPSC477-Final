Jabil reported a strong second quarter, delivering core earnings per share of $1.27 and revenue of $6.8 billion due to heightened product demand and solid execution. The revised outlook for the year anticipates a 25% increase in core earnings to around $5 per share, with top-line revenue approaching $28.5 billion. This improved portfolio mix will boost the core operating margin to 4.2%. Jabil is well-positioned in growth markets like healthcare, automotive, 5G, packaging, and semiconductors. The company's deep domain expertise, technology-enabled products, and commitment to sustainability have contributed to its success in these industries, which are expected to continue driving long-term growth. Additionally, Jabil remains committed to generating free cash flow in excess of $600 million for the year.
