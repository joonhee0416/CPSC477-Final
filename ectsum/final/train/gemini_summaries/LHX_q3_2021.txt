L3Harris Technologies faced supply chain disruptions in the third quarter, leading to a 1% decline in organic revenue to $4.2 billion. Despite this, the company delivered strong financial results with a 13% increase in earnings per share to $3.21. The company has adjusted its full-year guidance, anticipating a 2% increase in organic revenue. L3Harris is optimistic about its long-term growth prospects, supported by its leadership in space missile tracking, ISR aircraft, and other key areas. They are confident in their ability to deliver sustainable revenue growth, rising margins, and strong cash flow conversion. The company has also maintained its capital return program, planning for $3.6 billion in share repurchases and total shareholder returns of $4.5 billion in 2021.
