AMETEK had another outstanding quarter with record sales, earnings per share, and a strong operating performance. Overall sales were $1.44 billion, up 28% from 2020, with organic sales growth at 17%. Overall orders were $1.55 billion, an increase of 37% and the backlog is up $800 million from the start of 2021 to $2.62 billion. The company noted exceptional work, flexibility, and sustainability as reasons for their success. Earnings per share were $1.26, up 25% from the third quarter of 2020. The Electronic Instruments Group had sales of $982 million, up 31% over last year's third quarter while the Electromechanical Group delivered outstanding sales growth with an increase of 21%. For the fourth quarter, AMETEK anticipates an increase in overall sales in the low 20% range compared to last year's fourth quarter with earnings per share between $1.28 to $1.30, an increase of 19% to 20% over the same period in 2020. For 2021, AMETEK expects adjusted earnings per share to be in the range of $4.76 to $4.78, an increase of 21% over the prior year on a comparable basis. Their full-year sales are expected to be up in the low 20% range, with organic sales up by a low double-digit percentage over 2020.
