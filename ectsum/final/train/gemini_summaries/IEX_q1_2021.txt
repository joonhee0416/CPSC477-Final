IDEX Corporation exceeded expectations with a strong first-quarter performance driven by recovered end markets. Organic revenue growth reached 6%, contributing $13 million, while cost controls and price productivity added $9 million. The company announced the acquisition of Airtech Vacuum Group for $470 million, enhancing its offerings in high-performance blowers and pumps. IDEX increased its full-year earnings per share guidance to $6.05-$6.20 and organic revenue growth to 9%-10%, citing continued momentum and aggressive reinvestments. Despite challenges from safety protocols and supply chain disruptions, the company remains optimistic about its prospects. IDEX is also committed to sustainability, diversity, and inclusion, with initiatives to promote transparency, reduce bias, and expand supplier diversity.
