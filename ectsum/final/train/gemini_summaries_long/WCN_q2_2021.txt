Waste Connections reported strong Q2 results, driven by solid waste price plus volume growth (11.4%), which exceeded expectations. Revenue increased 17.5% YoY to $1.534 billion. The adjusted EBITDA margin also expanded by 110 basis points, leading to an adjusted free cash flow up 18.5% to $585 million. The company expects continued momentum and margin expansion in the second half of the year, leading to an increased outlook for revenue, adjusted EBITDA, adjusted EBITDA margin, and adjusted free cash flow for the full year 2021. Waste Connections remains optimistic about acquisition activity, with 14 acquisitions signed or closed year-to-date, highlighting the potential for significant growth in 2021.
