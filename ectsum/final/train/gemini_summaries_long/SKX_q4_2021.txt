Despite ongoing supply chain disruptions and COVID-19 impacts, Skechers achieved record quarterly sales of $1.65 billion in Q4 2021, a 24% increase year-over-year. Full-year sales reached $6.29 billion with strong gross margins. International sales surged 34% in Q4, contributing 65% of total sales. All reportable segments experienced growth, with international wholesale registering the highest gains. The company's direct-to-consumer business grew 30% with a 52% increase in international sales and a 21% increase in worldwide same-store sales. Skechers' comfort technology and innovative products resonated with global consumers, resulting in higher average selling prices. The company continued to invest in its distribution infrastructure, upgrading POS systems, launching new e-commerce sites, and expanding distribution centers.
