AmerisourceBergen delivered strong results in its third quarter, driven by continued execution, customer relationships, and specialty distribution leadership. Key highlights included a 17% increase in adjusted diluted earnings per share to $2.16 and revenue growth of 18% to $53.4 billion. The acquisition of Alliance Healthcare further strengthened their portfolio and global reach. The company also raised its fiscal 2021 guidance for adjusted diluted earnings per share to a range of $9.15 to $9.30, reflecting a 16% to 18% growth. Despite the challenges posed by the pandemic, the company maintained its focus on corporate stewardship, including talent development, community support, and environmental sustainability. Overall, AmerisourceBergen remains well-positioned to capitalize on market opportunities and deliver long-term value to stakeholders.
