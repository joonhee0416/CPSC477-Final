Reporting strong results for Q3, Rollins experienced a 4.9% increase in revenue, driven by residential pest control growth of 10.5%. While commercial operations continue to be impacted by COVID-19, steady improvements were observed as more businesses reopened. Net income rose significantly to $79.6 million or $0.24 EPS, a testament to the company's efficient cost management and successful execution across all its business lines. Rollins also highlighted its commitment to safety through stringent health protocols and the implementation of a disinfection service that has been well-received by customers. Importantly, two key executives, John Wilson and Jerry Gahlhoff, were promoted to Vice Chairman and President/COO, respectively, ensuring a strong leadership team for the future.
