Korn Ferry reported a strong fourth quarter with record revenue and profitability, demonstrating the relevance of its human capital solutions in the current business environment. Fee revenue reached an all-time high of $555 million, up 26% year-over-year, driven by growth in all business lines. New business also reached a record high for the quarter and accelerated throughout, positioning the company well for future growth. The company's marquee and regional account strategy continues to provide a steady stream of revenue, contributing 35.3% of total fee revenue in fiscal 2021. Cross-line of business referrals have also grown significantly, now accounting for nearly 29% of fee revenue in the fourth quarter. Adjusted earnings per share reached $1.21, up 102% year-over-year, with adjusted EBITDA margin remaining above 20% for the second consecutive quarter. The company has invested heavily in hiring additional fee earners and expects new business to remain strong in the first quarter of fiscal 2022, with projected fee revenue between $535 million and $555 million and diluted earnings per share between $1.04 and $1.14.
