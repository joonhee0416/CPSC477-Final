Rockwell Automation experienced a 9% decline in total reported sales in Q4 but saw steady improvement with organic sales growing 10% sequentially. Despite lower sales, strong segment operating margins sustained at 20% compared to last year, showcasing the company's increasing business resilience. The company expects organic sales to grow 5% in fiscal 2021, with reported sales growth of 7.5% at the midpoint of the guidance range, driven by a stronger second half. Adjusted earnings per share are projected to reach $8.65, an increase of 10% from fiscal 2020, supported by higher organic sales and cost reduction actions. Rockwell's strong financial position enables it to continue investing in strategic areas and maintain a solid capital structure, with free cash flow conversion targeted at 100%.
