**Spectrum Brands** reports strong financial performance in the second quarter, with revenue growth of 22.6% and an increase in adjusted EBITDA of 28.8%. This growth was driven by higher volumes, improved efficiencies, and continued investments in marketing and advertising. Despite inflationary headwinds that are expected to impact the second half of the year, the company expects mid-teens growth in net sales and adjusted EBITDA for the full fiscal year. Adjusted free cash flow is now projected to be between $260 million and $280 million. The company continues to focus on operational efficiency and has increased its Global Productivity Improvement Program savings target to $200 million by the end of fiscal 2022. E-commerce sales grew by nearly 43% during the quarter, and the company plans to acquire Rejuvenate, a leading household cleaning product manufacturer, in the third quarter.
