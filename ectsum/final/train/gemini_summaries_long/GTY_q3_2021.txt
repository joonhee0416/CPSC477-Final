Getty Realty Trust reported a 5.8% increase in revenue, a 13.2% increase in adjusted funds from operations (AFFO), and a 6.4% increase in AFFO per share for the third quarter of 2021. The company invested $61.1 million in 25 properties and $8.8 million in two additional properties after quarter-end, bringing its year-to-date investment activity to $144.5 million across 82 properties. Notably, Getty extended its $300 million revolving credit facility to October 2025, reducing its interest rate and improving its covenant provisions. The company's portfolio consists of 1,011 net lease properties, five active redevelopment sites, and five vacant properties, with an occupancy rate of 99.5% and a weighted average lease term of 8.8 years. Getty raised its 2021 AFFO per share guidance to a range of $1.93 to $1.94 due to its strong investment and capital markets activities in the first nine months of the year.
