ManpowerGroup reported stronger-than-expected financial performance in Q1 2021, with revenue increasing 1% year-over-year to $4.9 billion and operating profit improving 8% in constant currency. Demand for their services is increasing, as evidenced by an improved hiring outlook from the ManpowerGroup Employment Outlook Survey. All major markets, particularly the U.S., are showing positive hiring intentions. The U.S. segment experienced revenue growth of 23% to 27% year-over-year, while Southern Europe saw revenue improvement, with France estimating a 68% to 72% growth in the second quarter. Northern Europe's U.K. market is already performing above pre-crisis levels, with expected growth in the 30% to 35% range. The company expects Q2 2021 earnings per share in the range of $1.36 to $1.44, representing continued improvement. ManpowerGroup continues to invest in diversification, digitization, and innovation while maintaining a commitment to sustainability and ethical business practices, as evidenced by various industry recognitions.
