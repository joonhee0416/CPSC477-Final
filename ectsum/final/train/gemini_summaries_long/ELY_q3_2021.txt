Callaway's Q3 results showcased exceptional performance driven by strong demand across all segments. Topgolf outperformed expectations, with same venue sales reaching 100% of 2019 levels and adjusted EBITDA of $59 million. Golf equipment sales rose 8.4%, while apparel and gear increased 11.9%. Apparel brands TravisMathew and Jack Wolfskin showed robust growth. Topgolf venue expansion continued with two new openings and the announcement of eight new venues for 2022. Despite supply chain challenges, demand for golf remains high, with hardgoods retail sell-through continuing to trend upwards. The company anticipates growth across all business lines and regions in 2022, supported by strong demand fundamentals and no further significant COVID-related disruptions.
