DXC Technology reported improved execution in Q3 with continued growth in organic revenue, adjusted EBIT margin, and earnings per share. GBS revenue surged 7% with accelerating growth in Analytics and Engineering, while GIS revenue declined moderately. The company witnessed strong bookings totaling $5 billion, resulting in a book-to-bill ratio of 1.23 times. DXC generated $550 million in free cash flow, significantly improving from the previous year. The company has made significant progress in optimizing costs, with plans to sell non-strategic businesses for $500 million in additional proceeds. Its focus on building a solid financial foundation has led to debt reduction, lower interest expenses, and improved cash generation. DXC remains committed to investing in growth areas such as Platform X and ServiceNow, while enhancing operational efficiency and driving shareholder value through stock repurchases.
