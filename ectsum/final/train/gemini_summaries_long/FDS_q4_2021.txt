FactSet reported strong Q4 and full-year results, showcasing organic ASV growth of 7.2%. Research was the largest contributor, with a growth rate of 6% driven by strong sell-side growth and increased workstation users. Acquisition of differentiated assets, such as Truvalue Labs for ESG and BTU Analytics for energy markets, strengthens the company's competitive advantage. For 2022, FactSet guides for incremental ASV growth of $105 million to $135 million, representing a 7% increase and continued momentum in the business. Operational efficiency and cost discipline will contribute to operating margin expansion and accelerated EPS growth. FactSet is focused on enhancing client experience, delivering hyper-personalized solutions, and expanding into front office and ESG workflows to meet the growing demand for differentiated content and analytics in financial markets.
