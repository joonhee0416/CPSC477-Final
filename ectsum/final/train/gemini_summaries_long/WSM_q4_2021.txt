The company reported a strong finish to fiscal 2021, with a record-breaking Q4 revenue of $2.5 billion and a 10.8% increase in comparable brand revenue. Cross-brand customer sales contributed significantly to this growth, accounting for approximately 60% of total sales. The company maintained pricing integrity despite promotional activity in the quarter, expanding gross margin by 290 basis points. Operating margin expanded to 21% and diluted earnings per share increased by 37% to $5.42. The full year also saw impressive performance, with annual comparable brand revenue growth of 22%, operating margin expansion of 350 basis points, and earnings per share growth of 64% to $14.85 per share. The company's growth initiatives, including its B2B and global strategies, continue to gain traction and are expected to drive future growth. The company remains optimistic about its long-term outlook, reiterating its guidance of mid- to high single-digit comp growth and operating margins in line with fiscal 2021 levels. Capital allocation will continue to prioritize investments in technology, the supply chain, and dividends, along with elevated share repurchases.
