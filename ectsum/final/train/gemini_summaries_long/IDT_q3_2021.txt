IDT Corp. reported a strong quarter with a 16% year-over-year increase in consolidated revenue to $374 million. Despite excluding positive adjustments, revenue still saw a $52 million increase. All three segments (net2phone-UCaaS, fintech, and traditional communications) exceeded expectations. Net2phone's subscription revenue grew 39%, driven by API integrations. NRS's revenue soared by over 120%, boosted by payment processing and digital advertising. BOSS Revolution, excluding FX gains, saw a 63% revenue increase. Mobile top-up revenue surged by $36 million sequentially, contributing to a jump in adjusted EBITDA less capex for the traditional communications segment. These strong operational results improved the company's balance sheet and strengthened its available cash and current ratios.
