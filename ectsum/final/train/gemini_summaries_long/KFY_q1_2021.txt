Amidst the ongoing pandemic and unprecedented change, Korn Ferry remains optimistic about its future growth prospects. Despite a 28% decline in fee revenue in the first quarter due to temporary business disruptions, the company witnessed green shoots in new business, with July up 34% over June. They are reimagining their business through digital transformation, AI technology, and a focus on diversity and inclusion. Korn Ferry's strong balance sheet with $1.2 billion in liquidity provides stability to navigate the crisis and invest in the recovery. While monthly new business trends have stabilized and improved in recent months, uncertainty remains due to the unpredictable nature of the pandemic and rapidly changing global conditions. However, Korn Ferry is confident in its ability to help organizations and individuals succeed through its comprehensive suite of talent solutions.
