Despite ongoing supply chain challenges and inflationary pressures, the company reported strong financial performance in Q3. Label and Graphic Materials posted impressive topline growth, while Retail Branding and Information Solutions continued to expand margins. Intelligent Labels sales surged by 15%, driven by strong growth in RFID and non-apparel applications. The Industrial and Healthcare Materials segment also experienced a sales increase, despite inflationary headwinds. The company has raised its 2021 earnings per share guidance to $8.80-$8.95, primarily due to robust volume growth and higher prices. Adjusted EBITDA margin remained strong, supported by a combination of product reengineering and price increases. Despite inflationary pressures, the company generated $639 million in free cash flow year-to-date and anticipates exceeding $700 million in free cash flow for the year.
