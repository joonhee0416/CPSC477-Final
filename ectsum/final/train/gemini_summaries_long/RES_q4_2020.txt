RPC reported an adjusted loss per share of $0.03 in Q4, beating the Refinitiv IBES estimate of $0.07. Revenue for the quarter was $148.6 million, a 27.5% increase from Q3 and exceeding the estimated $120.8 million. Improved activity levels contributed to the revenue growth, particularly in the completion segment. RPC's operating loss narrowed, with adjusted EBITDA reaching $7.8 million. Cost of revenues as a percentage of revenue decreased to 79.3%, driven by higher revenues and improved labor efficiency. Despite increased activity, the company remains committed to capital discipline and expects to limit capital expenditures to essential maintenance and growth opportunities. As of Q4, RPC had cash on hand of $84.5 million and no debt.
