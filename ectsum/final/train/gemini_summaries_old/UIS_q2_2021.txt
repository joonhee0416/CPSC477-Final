Unisys reported strong financial results in the second quarter, including double-digit revenue growth and significant profitability and cash flow improvements. The company emphasized progress towards strategic goals, particularly in transforming its Digital Workplace Solutions (DWS) business towards higher growth and margin solutions. They highlighted partnership developments, acquisitions, and client wins within DWS, along with expanding ECS (formerly ClearPath Forward) services and enhancing license revenue. The company also cited success in expanding its cloud capabilities within the C&I segment, with revenue growing 28% year-over-year.

Financially, Unisys achieved a non-GAAP operating profit margin of 9.7%, a significant increase driven by gross margin improvements and cost efficiencies. Non-GAAP diluted earnings per share reached $0.68, reflecting a substantial improvement from a loss of $0.15 in the prior year. The company anticipates non-GAAP operating and adjusted EBITDA margins to be in line with the prior year period in the third quarter, with the fourth quarter anticipated to be the strongest of the year. Unisys reaffirmed its full year revenue growth guidance of 0% to 2% and reiterated guidance for non-GAAP operating profit margin and adjusted EBITDA margin. The company expects to be free cash flow positive for the full year 2021.
