ServiceNow had a strong start to 2021, with subscription revenue growth of 30% year-over-year, exceeding guidance. The company's focus on digital transformation, coupled with its innovative Now Platform, has led to strong customer demand. This was evident in the quarter's multi-product deals, as well as wins across various industries, including financial services, telecommunications, and healthcare. ServiceNow's investments in low-code app development and artificial intelligence (AI) capabilities are also paying off, as customers seek to enhance productivity and automate workflows. Additionally, the company's Vaccine Administration Management solution has gained significant traction, with over 100 organizations and governments worldwide using it to manage vaccine distribution and administration. These factors contributed to strong profitability, with an operating margin of over 27% and a free cash flow margin of 46%. ServiceNow raised its full-year 2021 guidance, citing robust pipeline generation and the increasing recognition of its platform's capabilities. The company remains committed to its path to $10 billion in revenue and beyond, driven by its strong business model and the expanding digital transformation market.
