Nu Skin's CEO outlined the company's multi-year transformation to become a leading beauty and wellness company powered by an affiliate opportunity platform. Key initiatives include "Empower Me" personalized beauty and wellness strategy, expanding the social commerce business model, and enhancing the digital platform, all of which aim to connect consumers with innovative products and drive growth. Despite headwinds in China and Southeast Asia, Nu Skin posted a revenue decline of 8.8% in Q3 compared to 2020 but grew 8.7% over Q3 of 2019. The company anticipates annual revenue growth of 3% to 5% and EPS growth of 8% to 11% for 2021. Nu Skin's strategic investments in social commerce and digital platforms, along with the launch of new products like Collagen+ and META, position it for long-term success in the evolving beauty and wellness industry.
