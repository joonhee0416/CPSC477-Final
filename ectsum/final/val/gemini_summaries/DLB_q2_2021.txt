Dolby's fiscal first-half revenue increased by approximately 10% compared to the previous year. Driven by the adoption of Dolby Technologies in various devices, the company witnessed strong performance in licensing revenues. Notable developments include the integration of Dolby Atmos for music into Lucid Motors' first automobile, collaborations with music streaming services to expand Atmos availability, and partnerships with gaming companies such as Microsoft Xbox to bring Dolby Vision and Dolby Atmos to gaming experiences. Despite uncertainties surrounding the impact of COVID-19, Dolby anticipates organic growth in the second half of fiscal 2021, supported by broader adoption in various markets and recovery in the cinema-related business.
