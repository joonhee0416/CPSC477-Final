FactSet had a strong start to FY2022, with record incremental ASV and growth in organic ASV plus professional services by 9%. Institutional asset management clients were the most significant contributors to this growth, driven by demand for differentiated content and workflow solutions. Despite increased expenses, FactSet's adjusted operating margin exceeded guidance at 33.6%. The company remains committed to its multiyear investment plan, with a focus on scaling its digital platform, investing in content like ESG and private markets, and expanding workflow solutions. FactSet's GAAP earnings per share increased by 7% and adjusted diluted earnings per share grew by 13%, driven by higher revenues and a lower tax rate. Client retention remained strong at over 95%, and the company added more wealth and corporate clients. FactSet reaffirmed its guidance for 2022, highlighting its early fiscal year momentum and commitment to delivering shareholder value.
