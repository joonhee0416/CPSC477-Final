Zoetis's second-quarter revenue grew 4% operationally to a range of $6.3-$6.475 billion, and adjusted net income increased 4%. Companion animal products drove growth, with 13% operational growth, while livestock products declined 5%. Despite the COVID-19 pandemic, the field force has returned to meeting customers in select geographies. The company raised its full-year 2020 guidance, projecting operational revenue growth of 3%-6% and adjusted earnings per share growth of 1%-5%. Zoetis remains committed to investing in manufacturing, information technology, and digital analytics, while continuing its 2020 dividend and share repurchase program. The company is confident in its diversified portfolio and resilience in the face of ongoing COVID-19 uncertainty.
