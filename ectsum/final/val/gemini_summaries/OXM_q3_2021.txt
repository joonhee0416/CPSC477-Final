Oxford Industries reported impressive financial results for Q3 2021, driven by its powerful brand portfolio and direct-to-consumer strategy. Sales increased by 41% compared to the previous year and 15% over pre-COVID levels, with direct-to-consumer sales growing by 40%. Adjusted gross margin expanded by 710 basis points to 62%, resulting in record earnings of $1.19 per share, up from an adjusted loss of $0.44 per share last year and $0.10 in Q3 2019. The company's Q4 outlook remains positive, with expected sales between $285-$295 million and adjusted earnings between $1.20-$1.35 per share. For the full year, Oxford Industries now projects sales in the range of $1.127-$1.137 billion and adjusted earnings between $7.52-$7.67 per share.
