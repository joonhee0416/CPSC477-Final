AES Corp. reported a 24% increase in adjusted EPS during Q2, exceeding $3 billion in revenue. The company's renewables segment continues to expand rapidly, adding a record 1.8 gigawatts to its backlog, with a total of 8.5 gigawatts under long-term contracts. AES Ohio and AES Indiana received key regulatory approvals to invest in smart grid initiatives and grid modernization programs. The company is on track to achieve its target of 7% to 9% average annual growth in adjusted EPS and parent-free cash flow through 2025. AES remains bullish on the future of renewables and energy efficiency, citing the global shift towards tackling climate change. With its proven track record, innovative products, and a robust pipeline of projects, AES believes it is well-positioned to drive superior returns for shareholders.
