Kimco's third-quarter results exceeded expectations, with FFO guidance raised to $1.36-$1.37 per share due to the successful integration of the Weingarten portfolio. Portfolio occupancy improved sequentially, with small shop occupancy rising 180 basis points to 87.3%. New lease spreads were a positive 5%, and renewal rates were a positive 4.9%. Same-site NOI growth was positive 12.1%, with redevelopments contributing 30 basis points. Kimco's balance sheet remains strong, with a net debt-to-EBITDA ratio of 6.3 times (pro forma) and $450 million in cash. The company opportunistically utilized its ATM equity program, raising $77 million in net proceeds. Albertsons' marketable security investment value climbed to over $1.2 billion, providing potential future monetization opportunities.
