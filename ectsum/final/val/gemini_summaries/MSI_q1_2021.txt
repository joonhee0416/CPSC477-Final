In Q1, the company achieved record sales, operating earnings, and cash flow, raising its full-year sales and earnings per share guidance due to strong demand and a robust backlog of $11.3 billion, up 8% year-over-year. The Software and Services segment contributed significantly, with 15% revenue growth and operating margin expansion of 310 basis points. Key wins included growth in video security, command center software, and LMR services. The Products and Systems Integration segment also saw growth in video security and professional radio, while facing supply constraints in public safety LMR. The company emphasized the strength of its video security business, particularly in fixed video and mobile body-worn cameras, and its continued focus on expanding into higher growth areas such as command center software and LMR services.
