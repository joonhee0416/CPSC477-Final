In the second quarter of fiscal 2021, Acuity Brands delivered solid results despite facing ongoing challenges. Sales declined by 5.8%, but gross profit margin expanded by 170 basis points and operating profit margin increased by 180 basis points compared to the prior year. The company achieved carbon neutrality in its operations and made progress in transforming its business, including leadership changes in its Lighting and Controls division and the introduction of new products. Despite supply chain issues and the impact of the pandemic, Acuity Brands remains cautiously optimistic about the future and expects improvements in its end markets. The company remains committed to capital allocation priorities, including growth through acquisitions and share repurchases.
