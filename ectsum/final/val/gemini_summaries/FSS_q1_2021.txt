Federal Signal reported strong Q1 results with record orders of $384 million, reflecting a 26% year-over-year increase. Despite pandemic disruptions and production challenges due to February's ice storms, the company achieved adjusted EBITDA margins of 14.8% due to strong demand. ESG contributed $228 million in net sales and $39.3 million in adjusted EBITDA. EFC experienced a 37% increase in orders. SSG saw its adjusted EBITDA margin improve to 16.2%. Despite a decline in sales, gross profit remained steady at 24.7%. The company maintains an optimistic outlook for the year, projecting adjusted earnings per share between $1.73 and $1.85 while acknowledging some short-term production challenges related to the global semiconductor shortage. Federal Signal is well-positioned to benefit from the American Rescue Plan and potential infrastructure investments, which could further boost demand for its products and services.
