Nabors delivered a strong finish to 2020, with adjusted EBITDA of $108 million, driven by exceeding activity expectations in the Lower 48, International, and Drilling Solutions segments. The company remains financially disciplined, reducing overhead by 24% and generating $184 million in free cash flow. Nabors's focus on premium assets and service expansion continues to pay off, with leading margins in its markets and the development of innovative solutions like SmartDRILL. The company expects to increase its rig count throughout 2021, supported by improving commodity prices and modest contract rate increases. Nabors is also embarking on energy transition initiatives, focusing on carbon footprint reduction and alternative power sources on its rigs. Despite the challenges of the pandemic, the company's resilient performance and commitment to operational excellence position it well to capitalize on the upturn in the oilfield market.
