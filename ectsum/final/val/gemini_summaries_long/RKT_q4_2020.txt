Rocket Companies reported exceptional financial performance in 2020, generating $16.9 billion in adjusted revenue and $11.1 billion in adjusted EBITDA. The company's scalable platform, which includes a national brand, a large sales force, and cutting-edge data science capabilities, has proven to be a significant competitive advantage. Rocket Mortgage saw remarkable growth, increasing closed loan volume by 175 billion in 2020, more than double that of any other market participant. The company's partner network has also expanded significantly, reaching $120 billion in closed loan volume annually. Despite the economic challenges presented by the pandemic, Rocket Companies has excelled thanks to its innovative digital platform and its ability to adapt and launch new businesses, such as Rocket Labs. To reward shareholders, the company has declared a special dividend of $1.11 per share, totaling an equity distribution of $2.2 billion.
