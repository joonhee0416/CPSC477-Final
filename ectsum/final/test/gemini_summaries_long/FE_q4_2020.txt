FirstEnergy reported strong operational performance in 2020, with GAAP earnings per share of $1.99 and operating earnings per share of $2.39, despite facing challenges and regulatory proceedings. The company resolved various Ohio matters with a charge of $0.15 per share, resulting in operating earnings of $2.54 per share if omitted. FirstEnergy has introduced 2021 operating earnings guidance of $2.40 to $2.60 per share, aligning with traditional utility growth rates. Management has implemented significant efforts to enhance compliance, including appointing a new Senior Vice President and Chief Legal Officer, Hyun Park, and establishing a dedicated Chief Ethics and Compliance Officer. FirstEnergy is committed to transparency and accountability, focusing on investing in its distribution and transmission systems, achieving carbon neutrality by 2050, and fostering diversity and inclusion. The company acknowledges the importance of addressing historical actions and rebuilding trust by prioritizing ethical behavior and compliance. FirstEnergy believes that ongoing efforts will strengthen their compliance program and culture of integrity, positioning them for long-term success.
