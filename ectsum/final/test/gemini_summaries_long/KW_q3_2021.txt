Kennedy-Wilson Holdings reported strong third-quarter results, with adjusted EBITDA increasing over 165% year-over-year to $203 million. The company's assets under management grew by 17% to a record $20.5 billion. Kennedy-Wilson completed $1.8 billion of investment transactions during the quarter, bringing its year-to-date total to $4.4 billion. The company's global apartment portfolio grew to 33,400 units, with plans to increase this to 35,000 by the end of the year. Kennedy-Wilson's co-investment portfolio saw strong performance, contributing $79 million in gains and $46 million in accrued performance fees during the quarter. The company has returned $750 million to shareholders since 2018 through dividends and share repurchases. The company's global portfolio continues to experience strong demand, particularly in its Mountain West region, where same-property revenue and NOI grew by 11% and 15%, respectively. Kennedy-Wilson's logistics portfolio has also seen significant growth, with a gross asset value of approximately $1.1 billion. The company has future commitments of $2.1 billion for its investment management business. With multiple initiatives for growth, Kennedy-Wilson expects to grow its stabilized NOI by 10-15% annually over the next three years.
