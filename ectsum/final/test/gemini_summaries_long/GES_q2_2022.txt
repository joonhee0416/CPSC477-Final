Guess Inc. reported strong second-quarter results, exceeding pre-pandemic performance with an 85% increase in adjusted operating earnings to $89 million. The North America Retail segment drove this success, reporting a 540% increase in operating profit. The company also announced a new $200 million share repurchase program and raised its long-term operating margin goal to 12% by fiscal 2024, along with an adjusted earnings per share target of $3.50. Despite ongoing challenges from the pandemic, Guess remains optimistic about the future, citing strong demand and store reopenings. The company is focused on key initiatives, including brand elevation through improved product quality and customer experience across all channels. They are also investing in omnichannel capabilities and data analytics to enhance customer engagement and maximize sales potential.
