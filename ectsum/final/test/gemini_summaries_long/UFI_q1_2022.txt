In Q1, Unifi's revenue surged by 39% due to robust sales and favorable mix. Despite cost pressures and supply chain disruptions, the company's strong global presence and focus on sustainability contributed to the growth. REPREVE sales rose by 39%, driven by increasing demand for recycled materials in various sectors. Additionally, the company witnessed a strong performance in Brazil, with sales increasing by over 50%. However, labor shortages in the US limited production, impacting potential growth in North America. Nonetheless, the company remains optimistic and has revised its full-year guidance, anticipating sales to exceed $750 million. Unifi is also investing in productivity improvements through its EvoCooler technology and implementing employee-driven initiatives to address labor challenges. Overall, the company's focus on sustainability, innovation, and a global approach sets the stage for continued growth.
