CIRCOR delivered another strong quarter despite supply chain challenges impacting revenue and earnings by approximately $10 million in Q3 and an anticipated $15 million in Q4. Year-to-date orders are up 11%, with Industrial organic orders growing 28% and backlog increasing 30%. Revenue rose 2% organically, operating margin expanded 80 basis points year-over-year and EPS increased 39%. The company also launched eight new products in Q3, including Hydrogen Isolation Valves and Modular Dome Regulators for the hydrogen market. Focused on long-term growth, CIRCOR is investing in margin expansion and automation, resulting in improved safety, quality, and productivity. Despite supply chain disruptions, the company's strategic priorities continue to drive growth, with Industrial organic orders expected to increase 10%-15% and Commercial Aerospace revenue projected to rise 25%-30%.
