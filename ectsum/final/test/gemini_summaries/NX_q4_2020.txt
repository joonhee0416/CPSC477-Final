During Q4 2020, the company's net sales increased by 6.3% to $851.6 million, driven by increased demand across all segments. However, full-year 2020 sales decreased slightly to $851.6 million due to volume reductions in Q2 and Q3 related to the COVID-19 pandemic. Despite lower sales, the company reported a net income of $22.2 million ($0.68 per share) for Q4 2020, compared to a net loss in Q4 2019. Adjusted EBITDA also increased by 14.3% to $39.4 million in Q4 2020. For fiscal 2021, the company anticipates net sales of $900-920 million and adjusted EBITDA of $108-118 million, buoyed by strong macroeconomics, robust activity in their markets, and a renewed focus on revenue growth.
