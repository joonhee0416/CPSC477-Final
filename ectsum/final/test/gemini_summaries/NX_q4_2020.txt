Quanex reported an increase in net sales by 6.3% in Q4 2020, driven by increased product demand across all segments. Adjusted diluted EPS for the quarter was $0.67, a significant improvement from $0.42 in Q4 2019. Full-year net income showed improvement, with a net loss of $46.7 million in 2019 turning into a net income of $38.5 million in 2020. Adjusted EBITDA also improved in both Q4 2020 and the full-year 2020. For 2021, Quanex expects net sales of $900-920 million and adjusted EBITDA of $108-118 million. The company highlights strong demand, improved operating leverage, lower raw material costs, and SG&A expenses as factors contributing to the financial improvements. Quanex remains optimistic about 2021, anticipating market tailwinds and operational execution to drive continued success.
