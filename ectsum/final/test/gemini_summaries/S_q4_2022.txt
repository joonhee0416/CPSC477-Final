SentinelOne delivered another strong quarter, reporting triple-digit growth in both revenue and ARR. ARR reached $292 million, a 123% increase year-over-year. The company added a record number of deals worth over $100,000 in ARR and closed its largest ever new customer contract. SentinelOne's profitability margins also improved significantly, with gross margins expanding by 12 percentage points and operating margins improving by 38%. The company highlighted strong demand for its cloud workload protection and data retention modules, which each experienced over 10x growth year-over-year. SentinelOne also announced its planned acquisition of Attivo Networks, which will expand its addressable market into identity security and add to its hypergrowth trajectory. Overall, SentinelOne remains well-positioned for continued growth in the cybersecurity market, as enterprises increasingly adopt its comprehensive XDR platform.
