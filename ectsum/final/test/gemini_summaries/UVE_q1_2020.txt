Despite the ongoing COVID-19 pandemic, the company performed well in Q1 2020, with adjusted EPS of $0.79. Direct premiums written grew by 15.7%, while net premiums earned increased by 5.3%. The combined ratio increased by seven points to 94.1% due to increased losses. Total services revenue increased by 25.6%, driven by commission revenue on ceded premiums. Net investment income decreased by 16.1% due to lower yields on cash and short-term investments. The company repurchased approximately 312,000 of UVE shares and declared a quarterly cash dividend of $0.16 per share. In terms of reinsurance renewals, the company has secured over 75% of its desired core first event reinsurance tower for June 1, with authorizations already received from reinsurance partners for the upcoming program.
