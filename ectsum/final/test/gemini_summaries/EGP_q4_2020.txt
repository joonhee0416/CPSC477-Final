EastGroup's fourth-quarter earnings exceeded guidance, with FFO per share up 8.7% to $1.38 compared to 2019. Occupancy averaged 96.9% due to strong e-commerce and last mile delivery trends. Releasing spreads remained robust at 15.4% GAAP and 7.9% cash, marking six consecutive years of double-digit rent growth. Annual FFO rose 8% to a record $5.38, exceeding pre-COVID forecasts by $0.08 per share. In 2021, the company forecasts FFO per share of $5.68, representing a 5.6% increase over 2020. EastGroup plans to focus on value creation through development and value-add investments, with $205 million in development starts planned. Despite pandemic uncertainty, the company maintains strong rent collections and a conservative balance sheet.
