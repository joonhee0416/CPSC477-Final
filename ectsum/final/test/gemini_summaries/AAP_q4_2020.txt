Advance Auto Parts reported strong Q4 and full-year 2020 results, demonstrating progress towards its long-term goals amidst pandemic challenges. Comparable store sales grew 4.7% in Q4, with an adjusted earnings per share increase of 14% to $1.87, despite COVID-19 headwinds. For the full year, net sales reached a record $10.1 billion, while adjusted operating income rose 4.1% to $827.3 million, yielding record adjusted diluted earnings per share of $8.51. The company remains focused on protecting its team members and customers, preserving cash, and preparing for post-crisis growth, which has resulted in significant improvements in team health and brand reputation. Advance anticipates continued momentum in 2021, projecting comparable store sales growth of 1-3%, adjusted operating income margin expansion of 60-80 basis points, and minimum free cash flow of $600 million. Additionally, the company plans to open 50-100 new stores as part of its growth strategy.
