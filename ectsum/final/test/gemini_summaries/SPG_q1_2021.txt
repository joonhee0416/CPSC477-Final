Simon Property Group reported strong first-quarter results marked by improved profitability and cash flow. Funds from operations (FFO) per share rose to $2.48, an increase of $0.31 from Q4 2020. Despite international closures impacting results by $0.08 per share, domestic operations showed resilience with 98% inline tenant rent collections. Occupancy remained healthy at 90.8% for malls and outlets, with leasing momentum driving 4.4 million square feet of signed leases. Average base rent grew by 60 basis points year-over-year, while pricing improved with an average opening rate of $60 per square foot. The company raised its full-year 2021 FFO guidance to $9.70-$9.80 per share, representing a 6.5%-7.6% growth rate compared to 2020.
