IDT experienced another strong quarter, with consolidated revenue increasing to $340 million and consolidated income from operations rising to $12.9 million. Driven by growth in Fintech and net2phone-UCaaS segments, National Retail Solutions (NRS) saw a 150% increase in revenue, while BOSS Revolution Money Transfer revenue rose by 73%. Net2phone-UCaaS subscription revenue climbed by 36%, aided by integrations with Slack, Microsoft Teams, and Salesforce. The company added over 1,500 units to its NRS POS terminal network, bringing the total to over 13,700 billable units and revealing a 50% increase in revenue per terminal. Diluted earnings per share surged to $0.51 from $0.04 in the previous year, highlighting the company's consistent growth and focus on expanding its higher-margin businesses.
