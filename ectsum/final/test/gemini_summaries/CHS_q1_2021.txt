In Q1, Chico's FAS experienced a 38% increase in total sales, with Soma's exceptional 65% growth driving this surge. Gross margin and SG&A expenses improved significantly, while cash and liquidity remained strong. Notably, Soma continues to outpace the market in intimate apparel and aims to become a billion-dollar brand by 2025. Despite a net loss in Q1, the company projects a 28%-34% year-over-year net sales improvement in 2021 and a gross margin rate improvement of 18-20 percentage points over last year. This turnaround strategy, which emphasizes digital transformation and product innovation, is well underway, with the goal of driving shareholder value over the long term.
