In Q2 2021, Alamo Group experienced significant growth, with a 29% increase in total net sales to $348 million. The Industrial division saw a 27% increase to $231 million, while the Agricultural division grew by 35% to $116 million. Net income doubled to $26 million, and adjusted net income increased by 73% to $23.4 million. Despite supply chain and labor constraints, backlog increased by 10% to $503.6 million, up 132% year-over-year. The company's strong financial position includes $38.7 million in reduced debt and an adjusted EBITDA of $155.3 million. However, material cost increases, inefficiencies, and unfavorable service sales mix have affected gross margin. The company has implemented pricing actions to mitigate these challenges.
