ITW reported strong Q4 results with revenue growth of 5.3% driven by organic growth across all segments except Auto OEM. Despite input cost pressures, the enterprise delivered operating margin expansion, leading to GAAP earnings per share of $1.93. Full-year 2021 performance included 12% organic growth, record GAAP earnings per share of $8.51, and a 7% dividend increase. Looking ahead to 2022, ITW anticipates organic growth of 6%-9%, 10%-15% earnings growth, and operating margin expansion excluding MTS. The company expects to return $1.5 billion to shareholders through dividends and share repurchases while also targeting divestitures to enhance organic growth and operating margins by approximately 50 basis points. Overall, ITW remains well-positioned to deliver solid financial performance and sustain its competitive advantages in the coming year.
