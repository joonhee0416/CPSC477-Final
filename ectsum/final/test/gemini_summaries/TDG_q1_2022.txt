TransDigm's Q1 fiscal results reflect positive trends in the commercial aerospace market, marked by a 10% increase in total commercial aftermarket revenue and a 20% sequential improvement in Commercial OEM bookings. Despite the pandemic's continued impact, the company's EBITDA margin remained strong at 47.3%. TransDigm is evaluating capital allocation options, including share buybacks, dividends, and potential acquisitions. The company is well-positioned for the remainder of fiscal 2022, expecting commercial aftermarket revenue growth in the 20%-30% range and commercial OEM revenue to grow significantly. Defense revenue is expected to increase in the low-single-digit percentage range. However, full fiscal 2022 guidance remains suspended due to ongoing market disruptions.
