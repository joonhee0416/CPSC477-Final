In Q3, the company reported record revenues of $755 million (up 29%) and cash earnings per share of $3.52 (up 25%). Organic revenue grew 17%, driven by a 22% increase in the Corporate Payments business. Sales surged to record levels, with 50,000 new business clients globally. Fuel revenues rose 13% organically, while tolls climbed 14%, and six million active tags were reached. The strong performance continues into Q4, with revenue and profit growth anticipated to be around 20% against last year. The company is optimistic about 2022 and plans to publish an ESG report soon.
