"1 Q1 Earnings Call Summary

Quanex reported strong first-quarter results for 2021, with revenue increasing by 17.1% compared to the same period in 2020. The increase was driven by increased demand across all product lines and operating segments, with the European fenestration segment experiencing the most significant growth. Net income for the quarter was $7.9 million or $0.24 per diluted share, up from $10,000 or $0.00 per diluted share in the same period in 2020. On an adjusted basis, net income increased to $9 million or $0.27 per diluted share, and EBITDA increased by 55.4% to $24.3 million. The company's balance sheet is strong, with a liquidity position of solid, and it expects to continue to be opportunistic with respect to repurchasing its stock. Quanex is raising its expectations for the year, with net sales of $945 million to $965 million, adjusted EBITDA of $112 million"
