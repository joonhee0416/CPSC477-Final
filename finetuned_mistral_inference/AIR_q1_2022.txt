"AR reported strong results for the first quarter of fiscal year 2022, with sales up 14% and adjusted diluted earnings per share up 206% compared to the same period last year. The company's commercial sales increased 52%, while government and defense sales decreased 17%. The strong performance was driven by the robust demand for airframe maintenance services, as well as the parts businesses, which showed an encouraging indicator of returning demand. The operating margin was 5.5% for the quarter, up from 2.5% last year and 5.2% in the fourth quarter. The company generated $18 million from operating activities from continuing operations and reduced the usage of its accounts receivable financing program. AAR also announced several new business wins, including an exclusive agreement with Arkwin to distribute engine actuation and other commercial aviation products, a contract with the Department of Energy for the conversion and delivery of a 737-700 aircraft, and an extension of its component support program with Volotea.
