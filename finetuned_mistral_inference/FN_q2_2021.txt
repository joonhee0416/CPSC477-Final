"1 Q2 Earnings Call Summary

The company reported record Q2 revenue of $453.8 million, up 34% from the same period last year. Revenue from automotive programs grew more than 30% from the first quarter and more than doubled from a year ago. The company expects to see continued growth in the automotive space in the third quarter. Revenue from industrial lasers was approximately flat, but is expected to improve in the third quarter. In optical communications, the company saw modest growth in revenue in the second quarter, driven by stronger telecom revenue. The company is optimistic about the longer-term potential for increased outsourcing from the industrial laser market. The company is also expanding its manufacturing footprint, with construction expected to take approximately 1.5 years and revenue from the new facility expected in approximately two years. The company's gross margin was at its highest levels in over a year, and operating margin also increased year-over-year and sequentially. The company is optimistic about the third quarter and expects to deliver another record performance."
