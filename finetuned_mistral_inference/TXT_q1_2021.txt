"1 First Quarter Earnings Call Summary

Textron reported strong financial results for the first quarter of 2021, with revenues of $2.9 billion and adjusted net income of $0.70 per share. The company's segment profit for the quarter was $256 million, up from $156 million in the first quarter of 2020.

At Bell, revenues were up in the quarter on higher commercial revenues, partially offset by lower military revenues. The company delivered 17 helicopters in the quarter and saw solid order activity in multiple end markets. Bell is currently working on the FLRAA program and has completed the V-280 demonstration flight program.

Textron Systems' revenues were flat in the quarter, but the business continued to execute well with improved operating margins. The company was awarded a contract modification of $293 million for the FLRAA program and successfully participated in the U.S. Army's Future Tactical Unmanned Aircraft Systems rodeo. Systems is currently responding to the FTUAS RFI and delivered the first RCV medium prototype"
