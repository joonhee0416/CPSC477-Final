"0 VITAS Healthcare Performance

VITAS Healthcare's 2020 performance was negatively impacted by the COVID-19 pandemic, particularly in the hospice industry. Despite this, VITAS was able to remain open and operate safely for patients, customers, and employees. The company's operational and financial performance was a testament to the success of its efforts to navigate the pandemic.

The pandemic disrupted traditional hospice referral sources, including nursing homes and assisted-living facilities. As a result, VITAS's average daily census declined 2.8% in the fourth quarter of 2020. Despite this, VITAS's hospital referrals have returned to pre-pandemic levels, and hospital-generated admissions increased 6.2% and 7.4% in the third and fourth quarters, respectively.
