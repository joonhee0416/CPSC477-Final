"9 APS Earnings Call Summary

In the 2019 earnings call, Arizona Public Service (APS) discussed its focus on four concepts that align with industry trends and shape the way the company does business. These concepts are clean, affordable, reliable, and customer-focused. APS has a goal to deliver 100% clean carbon-free electricity to customers by 2050, with a near-term target of 65% clean energy with 45% coming from renewables by 2030. The company plans to acquire resources that balance reliability, cost, and flexibility for customers. APS is committed to providing options that make it easier to do business with them and developing innovative programs that connect customers with advanced technologies to help manage their bill. The company plans to continue working collaboratively with stakeholders and maintaining an open dialog with regulators. APS expects to issue equity sometime after 2020, with an expected amount in the $300 million to $400 million range. The company's financial health remains strong, with a solid equity layer that provides customers with the benefits of low-cost access to capital"
