Getty Realty Trust reported strong financial results for Q4 2020, driven by growth in rental income and acquisitions. Adjusted Funds From Operations (AFFO) per share rose 12% year-over-year to $0.48, while total revenues increased 3.3% to $37.1 million. The company acquired 34 properties for $150 million in 2020, including 10 in Q4 that generated a weighted average initial return of 7%. Getty also completed six redevelopment projects in 2020, with one commencing rent in Q4 and delivering an expected return of 45%. The portfolio consists of 946 net lease properties, with 65% of annualized base rent coming from the top 50 MSAs. The company maintained a high occupancy rate of 99.3% and a low net debt-to-EBITDA ratio of 4.9x. Getty anticipates continued growth in 2021 and has established AFFO per share guidance at $1.86 to $1.88.
