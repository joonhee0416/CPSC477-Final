Advance Auto Parts reported strong financial results in Q3, driven by ongoing sales growth, margin expansion, and increased earnings per share. The company's net sales grew 3.1% to $2.6 billion, while comparable store sales increased 3.1%. Adjusted gross margin improved 246 basis points to 46.2%, mainly due to strategic pricing and sourcing initiatives. Adjusted operating income increased to $274 million, resulting in a 37 basis point expansion in the operating income margin to 10.4%. Adjusted diluted earnings per share rose 21.6% to $3.21 compared to Q3 2020. The company continues to invest in its team and business, expanding its store footprint, implementing new distribution centers, and enhancing its supply chain capabilities. Advance Auto Parts also remained committed to returning excess cash to shareholders, repurchasing shares at an average price of $205.65 and paying a cash dividend of $1 per share.
