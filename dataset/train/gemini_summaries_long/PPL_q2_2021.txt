Following the sale of its U.K. utility for $10.4 billion, PPL plans to repurchase $500 million in shares in 2021. The company also announced an ambitious new goal of achieving net-zero carbon emissions by 2050, with interim targets of 80% reduction by 2040 and 70% reduction by 2035. This goal reflects PPL's commitment to driving innovation and enabling a clean energy transition. The company also recently secured the necessary approvals to acquire Narragansett Electric in Rhode Island, which is expected to close next year. In the second quarter, PPL reported $0.03 in earnings per share according to GAAP, and $0.19 in adjusted earnings per share.
