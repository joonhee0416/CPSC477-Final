Barnes Group's second quarter saw strong year-over-year and sequential improvement, with organic sales up 31% and adjusted earnings per share surging 67% to $0.45. Notably, total backlog reached $984 million, reflecting a 12% increase from Q1. The Industrial segment performed exceptionally well, with organic orders and revenue growth of 37% and 42%, respectively, exceeding pre-pandemic levels. Aerospace also showed momentum, with OEM orders generating a strong book-to-bill of 2.5 times. For the full year, Barnes Group projects organic sales growth of 11% to 12%, driven by strong Industrial growth, and expects adjusted operating margin of 13%. Despite supply chain challenges and inflation, the company remains confident in its ability to execute its growth strategy and seize opportunities in recovering end markets.
