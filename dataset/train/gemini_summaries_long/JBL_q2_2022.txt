Jabil's Q2 performance exceeded expectations, with revenue reaching $7.6 billion, driven by strong demand in 5G, cloud computing, automotive, and healthcare. The company's diversified portfolio and exceptional execution resulted in a core operating margin of 4.6%, an increase of 40 basis points year over year. Additionally, core earnings per share reached $1.68, representing a 32% improvement. Jabil has raised its financial outlook for fiscal year 2022, anticipating revenue of $32.6 billion and core earnings per share of approximately $7.25, reflecting a 30% increase year over year. The outlook for fiscal year 2023 is also positive due to the company's momentum and presence in secular growth markets, such as electric vehicles, 5G infrastructure, and personalized healthcare. Jabil remains committed to generating over $700 million in free cash flow this year.
