In Q3, the company experienced its fifth consecutive quarter of double-digit top-line growth, with sales increasing by 11%, despite facing supply chain challenges. Operating margin was 17.5% due to planned increases in SG&A expenses. Plumbing sales grew by 15%, driven by strong demand in North America and international markets, and the company plans to invest $100 million in a new manufacturing facility in Serbia to support further growth. Decorative Architectural segment saw a 4% increase in sales, with propane experiencing over 45% growth. The company anticipates achieving price-cost neutrality by year-end, following mid-teens inflation in Q4. Adjusted EPS for the full year is estimated to range between $3.67 and $3.73, with sales growth between 14% and 16% and an operating margin of approximately 17.5%.
