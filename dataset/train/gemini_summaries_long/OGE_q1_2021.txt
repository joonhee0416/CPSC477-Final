OGE Energy reported $0.26 per share earnings for Q1 2021, driven by utility earnings ($0.06 per share), Enable earnings ($0.19 per share), and holding company earnings ($0.01 per share). Despite winter storm impacts, OG&E expects to remain within its guidance range of $1.76 to $1.86 per share, with mitigating measures already in place. Regulatory approvals for cost recovery and securitization in Oklahoma and Arkansas, as well as grid enhancement investments, position the company for sustained load growth of at least 1%. OGE remains confident in its 5% earnings growth target through 2025, supported by strong operational execution and attractive rates that drive economic development in its service territory.
