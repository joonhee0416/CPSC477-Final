CIRCOR continues to make progress in executing its strategic plan, with revenue in Q4 2020 reaching $208 million, up 10% sequentially. The defense business remains strong, with margins improving by 290 basis points year-over-year. Notably, aerospace and defense won 20 new programs in 2020, including 60 in defense and four in commercial. CIRCOR is also well-positioned for growth in 2021, with expectations of organic revenue growth of 0%-4% and adjusted earnings per share in the range of $2.00 to $2.20. The company's focus on value-based pricing, cost reduction, and operational improvement continues to drive margin expansion and free cash flow generation, which will be used to further delever the balance sheet.
