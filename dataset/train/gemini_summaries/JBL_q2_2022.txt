Earnings per share for the second quarter of fiscal 2022 reached $1.68 with a revenue of $7.6 billion. For the full year, core earnings per share are projected around $7.25, representing nearly a 30% increase year-over-year, while revenue is anticipated to be in the range of $32.6 billion, reflecting a 10% increase. The company's diversified portfolio, which includes secular end markets such as electric vehicles, personalized healthcare, and cloud computing, has contributed to resilience and stability. Despite global supply chain challenges, the electric vehicle segment has outperformed, with revenue growth exceeding 50%. Strong growth is also anticipated in healthcare, automotive retail, and 5G wireless markets. With continued strategic execution, the company is optimistic and expects to increase core operating margin to 4.6% for fiscal '22 and drive it beyond 5% in the future.
