For the second quarter of 2020, consolidated earnings fell to $0.26 per diluted share compared to $0.38 in Q2 2019. Year-to-date, Avista has earned $0.98 per diluted share compared to $2.14 last year. Guidance for 2020 has been confirmed as $1.75 to $1.95 per diluted share. Avista is in the process of completing the Catalyst building and the Scott Morris Center for Energy Innovation, which will allow the company to continue innovating and test new ideas in the energy space. Despite supply chain delays due to COVID-19, an estimated $405 million will be spent on utility infrastructure. Over 90% of utility revenue is covered by regulatory mechanisms. The company is committed to achieving 100% clean electricity by 2045 and 100% carbon-neutral resources by 2027.
