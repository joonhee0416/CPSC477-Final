In the second quarter of fiscal 2021, Cabot Corporation reported strong financial performance, with adjusted earnings per share of $1.38, up 79% year-over-year. This growth was driven by strong volumes across regions, favorable product mix, and disciplined operational execution. The Reinforcement Materials segment generated a record EBIT of $89 million, while Performance Chemicals saw a significant increase with EBIT of $58 million. Despite the ongoing COVID-19 pandemic and supply chain challenges, Cabot remains optimistic about growth opportunities in the coming years. The company also showcased its commitment to sustainability, completing an air pollution control project and continuing investments in growth areas like batteries. For the full year, Cabot projects adjusted earnings per share to be in the range of $4.70 to $4.95, expecting strong demand to persist in the second half.
