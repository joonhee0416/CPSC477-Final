The merger of SYNNEX and Tech Data has created a transformative entity in the technology distribution industry. This combination accelerates SYNNEX's strategic initiatives, enabling it to offer additional services and capabilities to partners. Through the merger, the combined company will have access to a larger global footprint, strengthened financial position, and enhanced product offerings. The portfolio will include over 200,000 products and solutions, allowing the company to transform value creation from a linear model to a multi-point model. This merger will also enable the company to capitalize on growth opportunities in areas such as cloud, analytics, IoT, and security. The company's Q1 results exceeded internal expectations, driven by strong demand across all businesses and regions. The company expects Q2 revenue to range from $4.7 billion to $5 billion and non-GAAP earnings per share to be between $1.80 and $2.00. Long-term, the company anticipates healthy IT spending, leading to non-GAAP earnings per share of approximately $8 for fiscal year 2021.
