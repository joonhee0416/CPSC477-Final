Getty Realty Trust reported a strong third quarter with a 5.8% increase in total revenue, 13.2% growth in adjusted funds from operations (AFFO), and a 6.4% increase in AFFO per share. The company continues to benefit from its stable portfolio of convenience and automotive retail assets, with full rent collections. Its investment strategies have contributed to earnings growth, with over $144 million invested year-to-date in properties including Flash Market and Splash Carwash. Getty has amended and extended its credit agreement, reducing interest rates and improving covenant provisions. The company remains committed to maintaining a strong credit profile and expects to remain active in pursuing acquisitions and redevelopments. As a result, Getty has raised its 2021 AFFO per share guidance to a range of $1.93 to $1.94.
