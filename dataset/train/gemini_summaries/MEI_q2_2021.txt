Methode reported strong financial results in Q2, with a 17% increase in sales to $301 million, leading to a 62% increase in net income and a 60% increase in diluted earnings per share. The Automotive segment set a record at $216 million in sales, driven by significant demand rebound. EV sales contributed over 9% to total sales, and annual expected sales from bookings totaled over $28 million. Methode's diversification strategy, including vehicle LED lighting and data center solutions, led to a number of key award wins totaling over $40 million in annual business. Despite ongoing COVID-19 uncertainties, Methode anticipates continued growth in EV and other markets, supported by a strong balance sheet and ample liquidity. For Q3, the company projects sales between $265 million and $285 million, with diluted earnings per share ranging from $0.69 to $0.85.
