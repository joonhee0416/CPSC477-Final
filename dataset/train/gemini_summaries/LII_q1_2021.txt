Lennox International reported impressive first-quarter results, with company-wide revenue surging 29% to a record $931 million and GAAP earnings per share rising 588% to $2.20. Residential revenue led the growth, climbing 37% due to operational improvements, favorable weather conditions, and a calendar-driven benefit. The Commercial and Refrigeration segments also saw strong revenue and profit gains. Adjusted earnings per share rose 305% to $2.27. Notably, the company lifted its 2021 guidance, projecting revenue growth of 7% to 11% and adjusted earnings per share of $11.40 to $12.00, driven by price increases, cost savings, and improved outlook for industry shipment growth. Further, Lennox plans to invest in research and development, IT, and factory automation while maintaining its commitment to share repurchases and expanding its Lennox Store footprint.
