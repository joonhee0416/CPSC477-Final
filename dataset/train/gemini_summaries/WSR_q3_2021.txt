Whitestone's Q3 financial performance demonstrated stable growth, with revenue increasing by 9% to $32.4 million compared to the same quarter in 2020. Same-store NOI grew by 7%, driven by occupancy increases and higher annual base rent per square foot. Funds from operations (FFO) core also improved by 9% to $0.25 per share. Occupancy reached 90.2%, with the acquisition of Lakeside Market in Plano, Texas. The company has made significant progress in reducing leverage, with debt-to-EBITDA improving to 8.1 times. Whitestone's strategic focus on entrepreneurial tenants and strong leasing activity resulted in a 5.4% positive leasing spread. The company remains committed to its dividend, with a payout ratio to FFO core of 42% and a yield of 4.3%.
