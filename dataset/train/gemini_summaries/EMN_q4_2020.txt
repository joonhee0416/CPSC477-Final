Despite COVID-19 challenges, Eastman Chemical Co. experienced a strong fourth quarter and robust full-year 2020 performance, including robust cash flow and resilient earnings. The company's commitment to innovation and market development has driven growth in its specialty products. Eastman anticipates a 2021 adjusted earnings per share increase of 20% to 30% compared to 2020, driven by market improvement, innovation-led growth, and improved mix. While COVID-19 uncertainties and rising costs pose headwinds, Eastman expects continued strong cash flow and a fifth consecutive year of free cash flow exceeding $1 billion. Additionally, the company announced a $250 million investment in a world-scale methanolysis facility, demonstrating its commitment to becoming a leader in the circular economy and creating value while addressing the global waste crisis and mitigating climate change.
