Envestnet reported strong results with a 23% increase in adjusted revenue. The company's market leadership, scale, and ecosystem of data, technology, and solutions are key drivers of its growth. Envestnet serves over 108,000 advisors managing $5.2 trillion in assets and processes millions of trades and service requests daily. Its MoneyGuide financial planning business and Envestnet tamp services remain market leaders. The company's continued investment in its ecosystem, including data-driven recommendations, is expected to lead to faster revenue growth in 2022. Envestnet raised its full-year outlook with adjusted revenues projected to increase 17% to 17.5% and adjusted EBITDA growth of 4% to 6%. Key financial metrics include adjusted EPS of $0.58 for Q3 and $2.30 to $2.35 for the full year.
