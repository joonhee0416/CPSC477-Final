IDEX Corporation reports robust first-quarter results, with a 10% overall increase in orders and sales, driven by strong organic growth of 6%. Despite the pandemic's lingering effects, IDEX is optimistic about its end markets, with most segments expected to fully recover by year-end. Notably, the company's Fluid and Metering Technology segment anticipates additional growth in the second half due to project-based businesses returning. IDEX has announced the acquisition of Airtech Vacuum Group for $470 million, expanding its pneumatics platform. The company has also raised its full-year guidance to $6.05 to $6.20 in adjusted earnings per share, driven by an expected organic revenue growth of 9% to 10%. IDEX remains committed to sustainability and diversity initiatives, promoting an inclusive culture and enhancing its efforts in these areas.
