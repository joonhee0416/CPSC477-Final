In 2021, Rollins experienced financial success with an 11.9% revenue increase in Q4 to $600.3 million and an adjusted earnings per share of $0.14. The company's termite service line performed exceptionally well, posting double-digit growth of 14.3% and significantly reducing damage claims to their lowest level in recent history. Rollins attributed this success to extensive reforms in their culture, training, and quality assurance processes. The company is also working towards resolving an ongoing SEC investigation, having increased its accrual related to a potential settlement to $8 million. Overall, Rollins remains committed to protecting customers and its reputation, and is confident in delivering on its short- and long-term objectives.
