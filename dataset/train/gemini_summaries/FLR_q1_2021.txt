Fluor reported adjusted EPS of $0.07 for Q1 2021, excluding NuScale expenses, foreign exchange fluctuations, impairments, legal costs, and an embedded foreign currency derivative. The company maintained its EPS guidance of $0.50 to $0.80 for the year, anticipating an uptick in awards and revenue in the second half. Energy Solutions contributed $60 million in segment profit, while Urban Solutions and Mission Solutions saw higher margins due to increased execution activity and performance scores. Fluor announced a $40 million equity investment in NuScale from JGC and plans to sell off its AMECO South American assets and Stork by year-end or early 2022. The company remains optimistic about energy transition opportunities and is pursuing projects in carbon capture, refinery efficiency, green hydrogen, and renewable energy.
