National retail properties company saw a strong first quarter, reporting a 98.3% occupancy rate, high rent collection rates (97% for Q1 and 98% for April), and an increase in full-year 2021 core FFO guidance to a range of $2.70 to $2.75 per share. The company's portfolio consists of 3,161 freestanding single-tenant retail properties with an average lease duration of 17.5 years. Despite two nonrecurring items totaling $5 million, AFFO per share was $0.76 for the quarter. With a strong balance sheet and no debt maturities until 2024, the company plans to primarily pursue sale-leaseback transactions with relationship tenants in the current low cap rate environment.
