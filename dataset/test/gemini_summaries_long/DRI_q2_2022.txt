Darden Restaurants reported strong financial results in Q2, driven by robust sales and margin growth. Comparable restaurant sales surged 34.4%, while total sales reached $2.3 billion. Earnings per share surpassed expectations at $1.48. Despite inflationary pressures of 6%, Darden's teams effectively managed costs through pricing and operational simplifications. The company expects full-year sales of $9.55-$9.7 billion, same-restaurant sales growth of 29-31%, and diluted EPS of $7.35-$7.60. Darden remains committed to investing in people, with plans to accelerate the minimum hourly earnings increase for restaurant team members to $12 by January 2022. The company is also on track to open 35-40 new restaurants this fiscal year.
