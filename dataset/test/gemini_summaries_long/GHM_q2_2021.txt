Graham's revenue rose to $28 million in fiscal Q2 2021, with stronger revenue and margins driving a $2.7 million net income for $0.27 per share, up from $1.2 million or $0.12 per share last year. Despite being impacted by COVID-19, diversification efforts have strengthened Graham's order pipeline, resulting in an increase in their annual revenue guidance to a range of $93 million to $97 million. The company's backlog remains healthy at $115 million, with 60-65% expected to convert to revenue within the next 12 months, providing a foundation for improved guidance. Graham's strong performance in the Navy market and ongoing efforts to increase productivity and reduce costs are expected to continue driving growth.
