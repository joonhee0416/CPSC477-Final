HII reported first-quarter earnings per share of $3.68, up significantly from $2.43 in 2020. Diluted earnings per share, excluding pension, were $3.56. Sales recorded $2.3 billion for the quarter, slightly higher than the previous year. New contract awards totaled $5.3 billion, resulting in a record backlog of approximately $49 billion. Key awards include a $214 million LPD program contract for Ingalls, a $3 billion CVN 74 refueling and complex overhaul contract, and a $175 million fleet to statement recompete for Technical Solutions. Segment operating income increased to $191 million, driven by higher risk retirement at Ingalls and improved performance in Technical Solutions. Net earnings reached $148 million, and cash from operations was $43 million. HII reiterated its 2021 guidance, targeting a shipbuilding operating margin in the 7% to 8% range and an effective tax rate of approximately 18%.
