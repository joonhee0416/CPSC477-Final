Federal Signal Corporation delivered an exceptional Q2 with strong financial performance and a record backlog. Consolidated sales surged 24% to $335 million, exceeding estimates. Adjusted earnings per share rose 19% to $0.50. Despite supply chain disruptions and higher commodity costs, ESG's adjusted EBITDA margin remained at 18%. SSG's performance was solid, with orders growing 39%. The company is optimistic about infrastructure legislation, which would support demand for its products in various end markets. Order intake was impressive, rising 79% to $361 million, resulting in a record backlog of $437 million. Federal Signal increased its full-year adjusted EPS outlook to $1.78-$1.90 due to strong demand and improved aftermarket performance in North America. The company is actively pursuing strategic acquisitions, and its strong financial position provides flexibility for further growth.
