Quanex Building Products Corp. reported solid financial results for Q1 2021, with revenue increasing by 17.1% to $230.1 million, primarily driven by increased demand across all product lines. Adjusted net income rose to $9 million or $0.27 per diluted share, a significant increase from $0.04 per share in the same quarter last year. Notably, adjusted EBITDA expanded by 55.4% to $24.3 million, representing an increase in margin of approximately 260 basis points. The company expects strong growth in 2021, with full-year net sales projected to be between $945 million to $965 million. Despite inflationary pressures and labor shortages, Quanex remains confident in its ability to achieve low double-digit revenue growth and maintain adjusted EBITDA margins in the low 12% range.
