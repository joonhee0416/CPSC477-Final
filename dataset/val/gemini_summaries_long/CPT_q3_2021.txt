Camden Property Trust reported another strong quarter with revenue growth exceeding expectations at 5.1%. The portfolio experienced double-digit growth in Phoenix, South Florida, and Tampa. New lease and renewal gains remain robust, with blended growth of 16%, while occupancy remains high at 97.3%. Same-property NOI and FFO per share guidance have been revised upward for the third straight quarter, with a full-year revenue growth projection of 4% to 4.5%. Camden continues to invest in real estate acquisitions and developments, with the recent purchases of two communities in Florida and Texas. The company's financial results for Q3 2021 include FFO per share of $1.36, surpassing guidance by $0.03. Full-year FFO guidance has been raised by $0.10 to a range of $5.34 to $5.40, with the midpoint at $5.37. The fourth-quarter FFO per share estimate is between $1.46 and $1.52, representing a $0.13 improvement from Q3. Camden's balance sheet is solid, with net debt-to-EBITDA of 4.4 times and total fixed charge coverage of 5.8 times. The company maintains liquidity of approximately $1.1 billion and $242 million remaining under its development pipeline.
