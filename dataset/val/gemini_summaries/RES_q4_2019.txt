RPC's fourth quarter revenues dropped significantly by 19.5% to $236 million due to lower activity levels and pricing. The company reported an adjusted loss per share of $0.07. Total impairment and other charges for 2019 reached $82.3 million, including $10.6 million recorded in Q4. The Technical Services segment experienced a revenue decrease of 38.7% compared to the previous year, with an operating loss of $17.2 million. The Support Services segment also declined, reporting a 13.2% revenue decrease and a lower operating profit. Despite the challenges, RPC closed several facilities, reduced headcount by 21%, and ended the year with $50 million in cash and no debt. The company estimates 2020 capital expenditures to be around $80 million, with a focus on cost management and operational efficiency.
