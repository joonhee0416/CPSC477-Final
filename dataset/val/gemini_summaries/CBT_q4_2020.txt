Despite a challenging year marked by the pandemic, Cabot Corporation emerged with adjusted earnings per share of $0.68 for the fourth quarter, showcasing a remarkable $0.75 increase quarter-over-quarter. This was largely driven by improved results in Reinforcement Materials and Performance Chemicals. Cabot remained focused on cash generation, delivering $99 million in operating cash flow in the quarter and $248 million in the second half. For the full year, adjusted earnings per share were recorded at $2.08. Despite the challenges, Cabot remained steadfast in executing its core strategy of investing for growth, driving innovation, and generating cash flow. The company's priorities for the upcoming year include staying close to customer needs, executing strategic growth initiatives, enhancing efficiency, considering bolt-on acquisitions, and maintaining cost and working capital control. With its strong balance sheet and experienced management team, Cabot is confident in its growth opportunities and ability to deliver on its strategic objectives. Furthermore, the company anticipates a strong first quarter with adjusted earnings per share in the range of $0.80 to $0.90.
