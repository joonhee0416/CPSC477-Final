CarMax's net earnings surged by 27%, with earnings per share rising by 27.9% to $1.79 in Q2. This growth was driven by strong performance across retail, wholesale, and CAF operations. Despite a weak June, used unit sales rebounded in July and August, leading to a 3.9% increase in total used units sold and a 1.2% increase in comparable-store sales. The company's omnichannel experience, which combines online and in-store options, has been well-received, with 70% of customers interacting with customer engagement centers and 50% progressing their sale remotely. CarMax is also investing in marketing to differentiate its brand and drive high-ROI customers to its digital properties. Overall, the company's focus on customer experience, omnichannel strategy, and investment in new initiatives positions it for future growth.
